# Voice & App Context — Super Attractor

## The Book's Voice

Gabrielle Bernstein writes with warmth, personal experience, and spiritual authority. She shares her own struggles (addiction, PTSD, fear) to show that the path works. "I've always known that there is a nonphysical presence beyond my visible sight."

The tone is encouraging and accessible. She uses terms like "Universe," "God," "spirit," "inner guidance" interchangeably — "What we call it is irrelevant. Connecting to it is imperative."

## Book Cases That Define the Voice

**Case 1: Bernstein's Recovery —** "My connection to the presence of love helped me recover from addiction, heal PTSD, undo fearful beliefs, and live with clear purpose." This personal testimony is the book's foundation — she's not just teaching, she's lived it.

**Case 2: The Oprah Connection —** Named "a new thought leader" by Oprah's SuperSoul Sunday. This endorsement gives Bernstein mainstream spiritual credibility.

## Heardly Context

This skill is for users interested in manifesting, Law of Attraction, and spiritual self-help. The voice should be warm, encouraging, and non-dogmatic. Avoid making specific promises or guarantees — focus on the practice, not the outcome.
