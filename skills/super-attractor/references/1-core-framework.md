# Core Framework — Super Attractor

## Introduction: You Are a Super Attractor

"Being a Super Attractor means that what I believe is what I receive. I can co-create the world I want to see by aligning with good-feeling emotions and directing them toward my desires." The book is about remembering your connection to a nonphysical source of power. Case: Bernstein recovered from addiction, healed PTSD, and built a career by tuning into "the Universe" — she calls this her Super Attractor power.

## Chapter 1: The Universe Always Delivers

The first step is claiming faith. "The Universe has your back." When you align your energy with what you want, the Universe responds. The key is not force but surrender — let go of the "how" and trust the "what." Case: "Tune in to this source of love has been my guide, my protector, and my partner in the co-creation of my life."

## Chapters 2-4: Aligning Your Energy

"It's good to feel good." Good-feeling emotions (joy, gratitude, love) vibrate at a higher frequency and attract more good. "There's more than enough to go around" — scarcity thinking blocks manifestation. "Have fun along the way" — manifestation should be joyful, not a chore. Case: "When you shift from fear to love, your entire reality shifts."

## Chapters 5-7: Surrender and Action

"Lift the veil" — see beyond the physical world to the spiritual reality. "Invisible guidance is available to you" — intuition, signs, synchronicities. "Do less and attract more" — the opposite of hustle culture. Surrender means letting the Universe handle the logistics. Case: "Manic manifesting" — forcing outcomes through willpower — is the opposite of Super Attractor energy.

## Chapters 8-10: Aligned Action and Appreciation

"Taking spiritually aligned action" — action that feels inspired rather than forced. "Appreciate and appreciate more" — gratitude is the highest vibrational frequency. "Let the Universe catch up with your dreams" — sometimes you manifest faster than you can integrate. Case: "Gratitude opens the door to receiving more."

## Chapter 11: Unwavering Faith

"The tough stuff offers a chance to shine the crystal that is me." Faith doesn't mean everything is perfect — it means showing up with faith no matter what. Case: "Being a Super Attractor doesn't mean everything is perfect — but it does mean I show up for life with faith."
