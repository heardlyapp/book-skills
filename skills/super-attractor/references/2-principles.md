# Key Principles — Super Attractor

## I. You Are a Super Attractor

"What I believe is what I receive." You have the power to co-create your reality by aligning your energy with your desires. This power is within everyone — it's not exceptional. Case: "Claiming my connection to the presence of love has directed the course of my life."

## II. Good Feelings Attract Good Things

"It's good to feel good." High-vibrational emotions (joy, love, gratitude, appreciation) attract positive experiences. Low-vibrational emotions (fear, doubt, scarcity) block manifestation. Case: "When you align with good-feeling emotions and direct them toward your desires, you attract what you want."

## III. Release Scarcity Thinking

"There's more than enough to go around." The universe is abundant. Scarcity thinking — believing there isn't enough — blocks your Super Attractor power. Case: "There's more than enough to go around — your desires don't take away from anyone else."

## IV. Surrender, Don't Force

"Do less and attract more." Hustle and force are manifestations of fear. Surrender to the Universe's timing and method. "Manic manifesting" — obsessively trying to control outcomes — pushes away what you want. Case: "The opposite of hustle culture is trusting the Universe."

## V. Invisible Guidance Is Real

"Invisible guidance is available to you." Intuition, signs, synchronicities, and inner knowing are real guidance systems. Learning to trust them is essential. Case: "The very fact that you are reading this book right now is evidence that you have listened to divine guidance."

## VI. Appreciation Opens the Door

"Appreciate and appreciate more." Gratitude is the highest vibrational frequency. It signals to the Universe that you recognize abundance and opens the door to receiving more. Case: "Gratitude is the quickest way to shift from fear to love."

## VII. Faith Is a Practice, Not a Destination

"Unwavering faith in the Universe" is not about never doubting — it's about returning to faith when doubt arises. Case: "The tough stuff offers a chance to shine the crystal that is me."
