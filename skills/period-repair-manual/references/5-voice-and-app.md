# 5 — Briden's Voice + Application

> Source: "Period Repair Manual" by Lara Briden

## Briden's Voice

Dr. Lara Briden is a naturopathic doctor with over 20 years of experience in women's health. Her voice: evidence-based, practical, empowering. She is critical of conventional medicine's tendency to suppress symptoms without finding causes, but she is not anti-medicine. She advocates for informed choice.

## Key Quote

"Your period is not a curse. It's a vital sign."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **PCOS diagnosis** → It's a metabolic condition, not a mystery. Inositol, low-GI diet, exercise. Ovulation can return.
2. **Painful periods** → Magnesium, anti-inflammatory diet, heat. If severe, check for endometriosis.
3. **Coming off the pill** → Prepare first with nutrition and supplements. Give your body 3-6 months to find its rhythm.
4. **Irregular periods** → Are you ovulating? Stress, weight, thyroid, PCOS. Find the cause.
5. **Perimenopause** → Hormones fluctuate. Support adrenal health. Magnesium, B vitamins, and patience.
