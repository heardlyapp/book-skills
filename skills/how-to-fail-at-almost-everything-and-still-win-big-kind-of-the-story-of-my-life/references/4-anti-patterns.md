# Common Mistakes — Anti-Patterns

**Anti-Pattern 1: Chasing Passion. Do not follow your passion. Follow your skill. Passion comes from success.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: Setting Hard Goals. Goals create stress. Systems create momentum.**

**Case: The Dream Job Myth. Finding your dream job is rare. Build a system that works regardless.**

**Case: The Grind. Working harder is not the answer. Work smarter. Simplify.**

**Case: The All-In Bet. Do not bet everything on one idea. Try many things.**

**Case: The Perfectionism. Perfectionism is paralysis. Good enough is better than perfect.**
