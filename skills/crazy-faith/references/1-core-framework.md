# 1 — Crazy Faith Framework: 10 Types of Faith

> Source: "Crazy Faith" by Michael Todd

## The 10 Faith Types (Chapters 2-11)

| Type | Chapter | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Baby Faith | 2 | New, fragile, needs nurturing. You're learning to trust God |
| Maybe Faith | 3 | "I think God can, but I'm not sure He will." Doubt mixed with belief |
| Waiting Faith | 4 | Trusting during the gap between promise and fulfillment |
| Wavy Faith | 5 | Up and down — driven by emotions and circumstances |
| Lazy Faith | 6 | Believing but not acting. Head knowledge without feet |
| Trading Faith | 7 | Bargaining with God. "If You do X, I'll do Y" |
| Fugazi Faith | 8 | Fake faith. Looks real but isn't. Going through motions |
| Stating Faith | 9 | Speaking promises into existence before seeing them |
| Fading Faith | 10 | Losing steam when the initial excitement fades |
| Saving Faith | 11 | Complete trust in Jesus — the faith that saves |

## Crazy Faith Defined

"Having the audacity to trust God to do what He said He would do, even when it doesn't make sense to anyone else."
