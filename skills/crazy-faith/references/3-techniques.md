# 3 — Techniques for Living in Crazy Faith

> Source: "Crazy Faith" by Michael Todd

## 5 Practices

1. **State it before you see it.** Speak God's promises aloud. Declare before you see.
2. **Take one step.** You don't need to see the whole staircase — just the first step.
3. **Wait actively.** Waiting faith is not passive. It's trusting, praying, preparing.
4. **Don't trade.** Stop trying to bargain with God. Obey without conditions.
5. **Keep going when the excitement fades.** Fading faith is normal. Push through.
