# 4 — Anti-Patterns: What Kills Crazy Faith

> Source: "Crazy Faith" by Michael Todd

## 5 Faith Killers

1. **Lazy Faith** — Believing without doing. James says faith without works is dead.
2. **Trading Faith** — Treating God like a vending machine. "I'll obey if You bless."
3. **Fugazi Faith** — Fake it. Looking spiritual on the outside, empty on the inside.
4. **Maybe Faith** — Staying in "maybe" forever. Never committing.
5. **Wavy Faith** — Only trusting God when things are good. Walking by sight, not by faith.
