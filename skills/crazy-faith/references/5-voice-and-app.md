# 5 — Michael Todd's Voice + Application

> Source: "Crazy Faith" by Michael Todd

## Voice

Michael Todd is pastor of Transformation Church in Tulsa, OK. Energetic, funny, relatable. He teaches Scripture with modern language and cultural references.

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Called to start something new** → Identify your fear → take the first obedient step → declare God's promise
2. **Faith feels dead** → Check if you've been lazy/trading/fugazi → return to obedience
3. **Waiting for breakthrough** → Don't waste the waiting → learn, grow, prepare
4. **Afraid of what others think** → Embrace the "crazy" → their opinion isn't your calling
5. **Doubt creeping in** → Doubt isn't sin — disobedience is. Act despite the doubt.
