# 2 — Principles of Crazy Faith

> Source: "Crazy Faith" by Michael Todd

## 7 Principles

1. **Faith is a decision, not a feeling.** Feelings change. Decisions hold.
2. **Obedience proves faith.** No obedience = no faith.
3. **Progression over perfection.** You don't need perfect faith — just growing faith.
4. **What's crazy to people is normal to God.** Embrace the awkward.
5. **Don't stay in one stage forever.** Grow from baby faith to crazy faith.
6. **Your testimony starts when you step out.** The story comes after the risk.
7. **God is more concerned with your character than your comfort.** Waiting builds character.
