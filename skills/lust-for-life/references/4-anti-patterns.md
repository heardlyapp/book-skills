**Case: The Starving Artist. Van Gogh did not choose poverty. He was trapped in it.**

**Case: The Tortured Genius. Van Gogh wanted to be well. He did not romanticize his illness.**

**Case: The Posthumous Success. Success after death does not compensate for suffering in life.**
