# Techniques: Reprogramming Your Subconscious

## Affirmation Formula

1. State your desire in the present tense: "I am prosperous" (not "I will be")
2. Keep it positive: "I am healthy" (not "I am not sick")
3. Use emotion: feel the truth of the statement
4. Repeat consistently: morning, evening, and whenever doubt arises

> **Case: The Insomniac's Affirmation** (Chapter 8): A man couldn't sleep. His mind raced with worries. Murphy taught him: as he lay in bed, repeat "I am at peace. My subconscious guides me to perfect rest." He stayed with the words instead of fighting his thoughts. Within a week, he was sleeping through the night.
> **Key takeaway**: Bedtime is the most powerful time for affirmations. As you fall asleep, your conscious mind relaxes its guard, and your subconscious is most receptive.

## Visualization Protocol

1. Find a quiet place
2. Close your eyes and relax
3. Create a vivid mental scene of your desire as already fulfilled
4. Engage all senses: see, hear, feel, smell, taste
5. Hold the image for 3-5 minutes
6. End with gratitude: "Thank you, this is now true"

> **Case: The Athlete's Visualization** (Chapter 9): Murphy describes an athlete who visualized his performance perfectly before every competition. He would mentally rehearse every move, every sensation. His subconscious accepted the visualized performance as real, and his body followed.
> **Key takeaway**: The subconscious cannot distinguish between vividly imagined and actual experience. Visualization IS practice — mental rehearsal that prepares the mind for real-world results.
