## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **Subconscious Mind** = Controls automatic functions, stores beliefs, manifests reality.
- **Conscious Mind** = Reasoning, rational, decision-making.
- **Affirmation** = Positive statement repeated to impress the subconscious.
- **Visualization** = Vivid mental images of desired outcomes.
- **Suggestion** = Planting ideas in the subconscious.
- **Prayer / Mental Treatment** = Affirming desired outcomes as already true.

## Key Principles

1. **Your subconscious accepts whatever you impress upon it.** It doesn't reason or judge.
2. **The conscious mind is the gatekeeper.** Guard your thoughts carefully.
3. **Repetition creates belief.** The more you repeat an affirmation, the more it becomes true.
4. **Feeling is the secret.** Affirmations with emotional intensity work faster.
5. **Opposite thoughts cancel each other.** Don't alternate faith and doubt.
6. **Gratitude accelerates results.** Thanking the universe as if you already have what you seek.
