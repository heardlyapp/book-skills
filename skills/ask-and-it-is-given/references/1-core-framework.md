# 1 — The Law of Attraction Framework

> Source: "Ask and It Is Given" by Esther and Jerry Hicks

## The Universal Law

The Law of Attraction is presented as an immutable universal law — as reliable as gravity. Like attracts like. Your vibration brings you experiences that match it.

> **Case: The Introduction** (Introduction — Teachings of Abraham): "Abraham explains that you are a physical extension of Source Energy. You came into this physical body to create, to experience joy, and to expand. The Law of Attraction ensures that whatever you give your attention to — wanted or unwanted — flows into your experience." The core teaching is established from the beginning.
> **Key takeaway**: You are not separate from the universe — you are Source Energy having a human experience. Your purpose is joy. The Law of Attraction is not a tool to manipulate the universe — it is a natural law that operates whether you're aware of it or not.

> **Case: The Vibrational Match** (Part I — Things We Know): "When you ask for something — by wanting it, talking about it, thinking about it — the universe immediately responds. But it doesn't send you the thing itself. It creates a vibrational offering. Your job is to match that vibration. When you do, the thing appears." Abraham describes the mechanism of manifestation.
> **Key takeaway**: The universe's response to your desire is immediate — it creates a vibrational match. Your work is not to make the manifestation happen but to raise your vibration to match what you've already received vibrationally. This is why feeling good is the most productive thing you can do.
