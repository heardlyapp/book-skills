# 3 — The 22 Processes

> Source: "Ask and It Is Given" by Esther and Jerry Hicks

## Practical Tools for Alignment

The book's most concrete contribution: 22 practical processes for shifting your vibration, organized by how easy they are to use and how quickly they raise your vibration.

> **Case: The Rampage of Appreciation** (Part II — Process 1): "Find things to appreciate. Start with something easy — the sunrise, a good meal, a friend's smile. Then add more. Keep going. As you appreciate, your vibration rises. This is the simplest and most powerful process in the book." The Rampage of Appreciation is the first and most accessible process.
> **Key takeaway**: Appreciation is the fastest way to raise your vibration. It's also the most natural — everyone can find something to appreciate. The Rampage is like a snowball: you start small, and it gains momentum. Five minutes of genuine appreciation can shift your entire day.

> **Case: The Scripting Process** (Part II — Process 12): "Write about your life as you want it to be. Use present tense. Write with detail and feeling. 'I am so happy and grateful now that...' The act of writing aligns your vibration with the reality you desire. When you read it back, you feel the feeling of having it." Scripting is one of the most powerful processes.
> **Key takeaway**: Scripting works because it combines attention (focus), emotion (feeling), and action (writing). It bypasses the logical mind's objections and speaks directly to the vibrational alignment. The more specific and detailed your script, the more powerful the alignment.
