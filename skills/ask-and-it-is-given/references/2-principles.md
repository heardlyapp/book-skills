# 2 — Emotional Guidance System

> Source: "Ask and It Is Given" by Esther and Jerry Hicks

## Your Feelings Are Navigation

Your emotions are not random or meaningless — they are a precise guidance system telling you whether you're in alignment with Source Energy or out of alignment.

> **Case: The Emotional Scale** (Part I — The Scale): "Abraham presents a scale of emotions from highest vibration to lowest: Joy, Passion, Enthusiasm, Satisfaction, Optimism, Contentment, Boredom, Pessimism, Frustration, Overwhelm, Anger, Insecurity, Jealousy, Fear, Grief, Despair. Your job is not to jump from despair to joy — it is to move up the scale one step at a time." The emotional scale is the book's most practical tool.
> **Key takeaway**: The emotional scale removes the pressure to feel good all the time. If you're in despair, you can't jump to joy. But you can move from despair to grief. Then from grief to fear. Then from fear to insecurity. One step at a time, moving up the scale. This is progress.

> **Case: The Indicator** (Part I — Guidance): "When you are feeling positive emotion — joy, love, appreciation — you are in alignment with your true Source Energy self. When you are feeling negative emotion — fear, anger, jealousy — you are not in alignment. The emotion itself is neither good nor bad. It is simply information." Abraham removes judgment from emotional experience.
> **Key takeaway**: Negative emotions are not bad. They are not punishments. They are indicators — like a dashboard light telling you something needs attention. The light is not the problem. It's the signal. Follow the signal to find the misalignment, and gently correct.
