# Core Framework — The Art of Manifesting

## Introduction: You Can Change Your Life

Carolyn Boyes discovered manifesting when she was living abroad, unhappy, wanting big changes. She wrote down "intentional manifests" — forgotten them — and 18 months later everything she'd written had come true. "I had the exact amount of money in my bank account I had written down a year and a half previously." Case: Her own story is the book's proof.

## Chapter 1: Awaken to Your Power

"All the power you will ever need to create a happy life is here right now. It is not outside but inside you." The Truman Show analogy — we're all in a dream until we choose to wake up and exercise free will. Case: Roshana in the café — unhappy with her life, feeling trapped — represents everyone who needs to wake up to their power.

## Chapters 2-4: The Core Method

**Desire (Ch 2):** "Desire is the fuel." You must want it — not just think it would be nice. Real desire creates the energy for manifestation. **Imagination (Ch 3):** "Create a powerful picture in your mind." Vivid, specific, emotional visualization. **Relax (Ch 4):** "Relax and release the outcome." Tension blocks manifestation. Surrender is essential.

## Chapters 5-7: The Follow-Through

**Commitment (Ch 5):** "Commitment turns desire into reality." Decide, don't just wish. **Belief (Ch 6):** "You must believe it's possible." Limiting beliefs block everything. **Habit (Ch 7):** "Make manifesting a daily practice." Consistency matters more than intensity.

## Chapter 8: Manifest

Bringing it all together. The complete manifesting method: 1) Clarify desire, 2) Visualize vividly, 3) Feel the feeling, 4) Release to the universe, 5) Take inspired action, 6) Give thanks, 7) Repeat. Case: "You choose which doors you are going to open."
