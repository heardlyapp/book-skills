# Principles · Planning · Persistence · The Subconscious

> Source: *Think and Grow Rich*, Chapters 8-12

## Organized Planning
A definite plan must be practical. Partner with a Mastermind group. If one plan fails, try another. Failure is temporary.

**Leadership qualities:** Self-confidence, justice, decision, planning, habit of doing more than paid for, pleasant personality, sympathy, cooperation.

## Decision
Successful people make decisions promptly and change them slowly. Indecision is a major cause of failure.

## Persistence
Created by: 1) Definiteness of purpose. 2) Desire. 3) Self-reliance. 4) Definite plan. 5) Accurate knowledge. 6) Cooperation. 7) Willpower. 8) Habit.

**Fear of criticism** is the biggest killer of persistence. Most people quit because they care too much about what others think.
