# Core Framework · 13 Steps to Riches

> Source: *Think and Grow Rich*, Chapters 1-7

---

## Step 1: Burning Desire
" **No one is ever defeated until defeat is accepted as a reality.** " A desire must be specific, powerful, and consuming.

**The 6-step method:** 1) Know exactly what you want. 2) Know what you'll give in return. 3) Set a definite date. 4) Create a definite plan. 5) Write it all down. 6) Read it aloud twice daily.

## Step 2: Faith
Faith is the head chemist of the mind. It can be created through autosuggestion — repeating affirmations until the subconscious accepts them as real.

## Step 3: Autosuggestion
Use the principle of auto-suggestion to reach your subconscious. Read your written statement aloud with emotion and feeling.

## Step 4: Specialized Knowledge
General knowledge is not power. Specialized knowledge, organized and intelligently directed, is power.

## Step 5: Imagination
There are two forms: Synthetic (rearranging existing ideas) and Creative (channeling from the Infinite Intelligence).

## Step 6: Organized Planning
A desire needs a practical plan. Ally yourself with a Mastermind group to create and execute it.

## Step 7: Decision
Analysis of 500+ successful people: they all made decisions quickly and changed them slowly. Unsuccessful people decide slowly and change quickly.

## Step 8: Persistence
"Persistence is to the character of man as carbon is to steel." Persistence is simply willpower in action.

## Step 9: Power of the Mastermind
"Two or more minds working in harmony toward a common goal create a third, invisible, intangible force."
