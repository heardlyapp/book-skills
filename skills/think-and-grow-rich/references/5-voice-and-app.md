# Voice · Key Quotes

> Source: *Think and Grow Rich*

## Hill's Voice
1. **Philosophical authority** — "I was commissioned by Andrew Carnegie to study successful people."
2. **Emotional, not academic** — Written to inspire action, not to impress scholars.
3. **Repetitive for emphasis** — Core ideas (burning desire, definite purpose) repeated until they sink in.
4. **Mystical elements** — "Infinite Intelligence," "the unknown self," telepathy — written for 1937 audience.

## Key Quotes
> "Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve."
> "Strength and growth come only through continuous effort and struggle."
> "Desire is the starting point of all achievement, not a hope, not a wish, but a keen pulsating desire which transcends everything."
> "The person who stops studying merely because they have finished school is hopelessly out of date."
> "More gold has been mined from the thoughts of men than has been taken from the earth."
