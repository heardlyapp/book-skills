# Techniques · The Mastermind · Fear Conquering

> Source: *Think and Grow Rich*, Chapters 10, 13-14

## The Mastermind Alliance
"Two or more minds working in harmony create a third mind."

**How to create one:** 1) Define your purpose. 2) Select people with complementary skills. 3) Meet regularly with a definite agenda. 4) Give as much as you receive.

## The 6 Basic Fears
1. **Fear of Poverty** — the most destructive. Symptoms: indifference, indecision, doubt, worry, over-caution, procrastination.
2. **Fear of Criticism** — kills initiative. Symptoms: self-consciousness, lack of poise, inferiority complex.
3. **Fear of Ill Health** — hypochondria.
4. **Fear of Loss of Love** — jealousy.
5. **Fear of Old Age** — the "complex of inferiority."
6. **Fear of Death** — the most fundamental.
