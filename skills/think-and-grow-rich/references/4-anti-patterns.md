# Anti-Patterns · The 6 Fears · Failure Mindsets

> Source: *Think and Grow Rich*

## The Major Anti-Pattern: Fear
All fear is the fear of something unknown. The 6 basic fears can be conquered by replacing them with burning desire.

## Failure Excuses
Hill listed 30+ common excuses people use for failure: "If only I had education" / "If only I had money" / "If only I had time" / "If only I was younger/older" / "I was born poor" — each one is a choice, not a destiny.

## Lack of a Definite Purpose
The single biggest cause of failure: not knowing exactly what you want.

## Procrastination
"The habit of putting off action is the most damaging of all habits."
