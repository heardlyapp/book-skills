# Think and Grow Rich · TAGR

Based on Napoleon Hill's *Think and Grow Rich* (1937, originally commissioned by Andrew Carnegie).
This is not a get-rich-quick book — it is a **philosophy of achievement** based on studying 500+ successful people.

## Philosophy
1. **Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve.** Thoughts are things — powerful forces that shape reality.
2. **A burning desire is the starting point.** Not a wish, not a hope — an obsession that consumes you.
3. **Faith is the head chemist.** Belief in yourself, combined with visualization, creates the neural pathways to success.
4. **Persistence is the difference between success and failure.** Most people quit right before the breakthrough.
5. **No one succeeds alone.** The Mastermind Alliance: two or more minds working in harmony toward a common goal.

## Framework Quick Ref
- **13 Steps to Riches**: Desire → Faith → Autosuggestion → Specialized Knowledge → Imagination → Organized Planning → Decision → Persistence → Power of the Mastermind → Transmutation → The Subconscious Mind → The Brain → The Sixth Sense
- **6 Basic Fears**: Poverty, Criticism, Ill Health, Loss of Love, Old Age, Death
- **Mastermind**: Harmony of minds = access to the "Infinite Intelligence"

## Anti-Patterns
