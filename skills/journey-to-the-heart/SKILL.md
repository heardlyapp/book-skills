## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **The Soul Knows the Way** — Your deepest self already knows what you need. Meditation helps you hear it.
- **Daily Practice** — Spiritual growth is not a destination. It is a daily practice of showing up.
- **Creativity as Prayer** — Creative expression is a form of spiritual connection. Make something every day.
- **Letting Go** — Healing comes from releasing what no longer serves you, not from holding on tighter.
- **Trusting the Process** — The path reveals itself as you walk it. You do not need to see the whole road.

## Key Principles

1. **Show up every day** — Spiritual growth happens through daily practice, not occasional breakthroughs.
2. **Trust your inner voice** — It knows what you need before your mind figures it out.
3. **Create something** — Making art, writing, cooking, or gardening connects you to your soul.
4. **Let go of outcomes** — Attach to the practice, not the result. The results take care of themselves.
5. **Healing takes time** — Be patient with yourself. The soul heals at its own pace.
6. **You are enough** — You do not need to become someone else. You need to become more fully yourself.

## Anti-Pattern Summary

**The waiting trap:** Believing you need to be ready, healed, or worthy before you begin. You begin, and the readiness follows. The path is not something you prepare for. It is something you walk.

## Self-Check: Recall Test

1. "How do I start a meditation practice?" — Pick one meditation from the book. Read it slowly. Sit with it for five minutes. Repeat tomorrow.
2. "How do I trust my inner voice?" — Start with small things. Trust your intuition about what to eat, where to walk, who to call. Build trust through practice.
3. "I feel stuck creatively. What do I do?" — Make something small. A sentence. A drawing. A meal. The act of creation unlocks more creation.
4. "How do I heal from the past?" — Acknowledge the pain. Feel it. Then release it. You do not need to carry it forever.
5. "What does spiritual growth look like?" — It looks like showing up every day, being kind to yourself, and trusting the process.
