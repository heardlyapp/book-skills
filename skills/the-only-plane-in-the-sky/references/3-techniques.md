# 3 — First Responders

> Source: "The Only Plane in the Sky" by Garrett M. Graff

## The FDNY

343 firefighters died. They climbed the stairs while everyone else was coming down. They didn't know the towers would fall.

## The NYPD and Port Authority

23 NYPD officers died. 37 Port Authority police officers died. They helped evacuate and established command posts.

> **Case: Climbing the Stairs** (Chapter on FDNY): "We were climbing. 30 floors. 40 floors. People were coming down past us. Some were crying. Some were quiet. We kept climbing. We didn't know what we would find at the top. But we knew people needed help."
> **Key takeaway**: The firefighters of 9/11 represent the best of humanity. They walked into hell because that was their job. Their courage is beyond words.

> **Case: The Collapse** (Chapter on FDNY): "When the South Tower fell, the sound was like nothing I've ever heard. We were in the North Tower lobby. We ran. The dust cloud was like a wall. We couldn't see. We couldn't breathe. We thought we were going to die."
> **Key takeaway**: The firefighters who survived the collapse did so by instinct and luck. Many of their brothers did not. The loss of 343 firefighters in one day is still the deadliest day in American law enforcement history.
