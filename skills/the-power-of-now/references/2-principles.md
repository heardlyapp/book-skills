# Principles · Presence · Time · Acceptance

> Source: *The Power of Now*, Chapters 3-5

---

## Moving Deeply into the Now (Chapters 3-4)

**Core insight:** Nothing exists outside the Now. The past is a memory arising Now. The future is an anticipation arising Now.

> *"Time isn't precious at all. The most precious thing there is is the Now."*

**Psychological time vs Clock time:**
- **Clock time** — practical, necessary for daily life (appointments, planning)
- **Psychological time** — dwelling on past regrets, anxiety about future, living in "what if" and "if only"

**The Practice:** Whenever you feel negativity arising, ask: "What's my problem right now — in this moment?" Usually, the answer is: nothing. The problem exists only in psychological time.

> *"All problems are illusions of the mind."*

---

## The State of Presence (Chapter 5)

**Core insight:** Presence is not what you think it is. It's not thinking — it's awareness prior to thought.

**Characteristics of presence:**
- Intense alertness without mental commentary
- The feeling of "I am" without adding anything to it
- Stillness within — even as you act
- Perception without the filter of the past

**The Practice:** Throughout the day, briefly pause. Close your eyes for a minute. Feel your breath. Sense the aliveness in your hands, arms, feet. This sends you into presence.

> *"Presence is needed to become aware of the beauty, the majesty, the sacredness of nature."*
