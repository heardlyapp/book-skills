# Techniques · Inner Body · Relationships · Portals

> Source: *The Power of Now*, Chapters 6-7, 8

---

## The Inner Body (Chapter 6)

**Core insight:** Your physical body is a doorway to presence. Sensing the inner energy field of the body leads you out of the mind.

**The Practice:**
1. Close your eyes. Feel your breath entering and leaving your body.
2. Direct attention into your hands. Feel the aliveness, the tingling.
3. Move awareness through your arms, torso, legs, feet.
4. Feel the whole body as a single energy field.
5. This is your "inner body" — your link to the Unmanifested.

> *"The body that you can see and touch is only a thin illusionic veil. It is the inner body that is your doorway to Being."*

---

## Enlightened Relationships (Chapter 8)

**Core insight:** Most relationships are driven by ego: needing the other to complete you, to validate you, to make you feel whole. This is addiction, not love.

**The Practice in relationships:**
- Observe when your ego uses the relationship to get something (attention, validation, security)
- Create space for the other person to be as they are — don't try to fix or change them
- Let them be. Your presence gives them the space to change themselves

> *"If you can't be at ease with someone when they are in a bad mood, when they are not giving you what you want — then you are not in love, you are in addiction."*

## Portals (Chapter 7)

**Gateways to the Unmanifested:**
- **Silence** — listen to the silence beneath sound
- **Space** — perceive the space between objects, the space within
- **Cessation of thought** — the gaps in the mental stream
- **Deep sleep** — dreamless sleep is contact with the Unmanifested
- **Conscious death** — surrendering into death as the ultimate portal
