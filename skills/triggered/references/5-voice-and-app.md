# 5 — Trump Jr.'s Voice + Application

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## Voice

Donald Trump Jr. is a businessman and political activist. His voice: combative, humorous, unapologetic. He writes for conservatives who feel silenced.

> **Case: The Author's Experience** (Introduction): Trump Jr. describes being "triggered" by the left's attacks on his father and family. "They attack our family, our business, our country. And they expect us to be polite. No. I'm triggered."
> **Key takeaway**: Trump Jr. writes from personal experience. His family has been the target of the very tactics he describes.

## 5 Scenarios

1. **You feel silenced** → Speak anyway. Your voice matters. Don't let fear win.
2. **You see media bias** → Don't trust any single source. Verify. Seek out multiple perspectives.
3. **A young person is being indoctrinated** → Teach them to question everything. Free speech matters.
4. **You're attacked online** → Don't apologize. Don't engage. Use humor. Move on.
5. **You're afraid to speak up** → Find others who share your views. Strength in numbers.
