# 1 — Cancel Culture

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Framework

Cancel culture is a system of social punishment. Anyone can be "canceled" for any perceived violation of progressive orthodoxy. The punishment: public shaming, job loss, social exclusion.

> **Case: The Deplatformed Speaker** (Chapter on Campus): Trump Jr. describes conservative speakers being shouted down, disinvited, or violently prevented from speaking on college campuses. "They claim to be tolerant, but they won't allow a conservative to speak. This is not tolerance — it's authoritarianism."
> **Key takeaway**: The left's claim to tolerance is false. They will silence any voice they disagree with.

> **Case: The Fired Employee** (Chapter on Social Media): "People lose their jobs every day for expressing conservative views. A private social media post, a joke with friends, a like on the wrong page — and you're fired." Trump Jr. argues that this creates a chilling effect.
> **Key takeaway**: Cancel culture is not just about celebrities. Ordinary people lose their livelihoods for expressing mainstream conservative opinions.
