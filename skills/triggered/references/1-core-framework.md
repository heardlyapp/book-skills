# 1 — Cancel Culture and Free Speech

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Framework

Cancel culture is a system of social punishment for violating progressive norms. Anyone can be "canceled" for any perceived offense — a tweet, a joke, a wrong opinion. The punishment is severe: public shaming, job loss, social exclusion.

## The Chilling Effect

The fear of being canceled has a chilling effect on speech. People self-censor because they're afraid of the consequences.

> **Case: The Deplatformed Speaker** (Chapter on Campus): Trump Jr. describes conservative speakers being shouted down, disinvited, or violently prevented from speaking on college campuses. "They claim to be tolerant, but they won't allow a conservative to speak within earshot of their campus. This is not tolerance — it is authoritarianism."
> **Key takeaway**: The left's claim to value diversity and inclusion is false when it comes to political diversity. They will actively silence any voice they disagree with.

> **Case: The Fired Employee** (Chapter on Social Media): "Ordinary people lose their jobs every day for expressing conservative views. A private social media post, a joke with friends, a like on the wrong page — and you are fired, and your reputation is destroyed." Trump Jr. argues that this is not a series of isolated incidents but a coordinated cultural phenomenon.
> **Key takeaway**: Cancel culture is not just about celebrities or politicians. Ordinary people lose their livelihoods for expressing mainstream conservative opinions. This creates a pervasive atmosphere of fear.
