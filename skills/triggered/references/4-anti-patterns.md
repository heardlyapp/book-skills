> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## Anti-Pattern: PC as Control

Political correctness is not about politeness or inclusion — it is a system of control. It punishes dissent and enforces progressive orthodoxy.

## Anti-Pattern: Impossible Standards

The left creates standards that are impossible to meet. This ensures that everyone is guilty of something. The goal is not justice — it is control.

> **Case: The Pronunciation Police** (Chapter on PC): "Now you cannot even pronounce someone's name without being accused of racism. The left has created a system where any misstep — real or perceived — is evidence of your inherent evil. There is no good faith, no benefit of the doubt."
> **Key takeaway**: PC culture is a weapon, not a virtue. The standards are designed to be impossible to meet, which is the point. Everyone can be accused of something.

> **Case: The Never-Enough Apology** (Chapter on Apologies): "When a conservative gets in trouble, the left demands a groveling, tearful apology. Then they attack them anyway. The apology is never enough. The goal is not reconciliation — it is destruction." Trump Jr. advises: never apologize to the mob.
> **Key takeaway**: Apologizing to the mob does not work. It only validates their power. The only winning move is to refuse to play their game.
