# 3 — Campus Politics and Generational Change

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Indoctrination

Trump Jr. argues that universities have been captured by the left and are now centers of progressive indoctrination rather than education.

## Safe Spaces and Trigger Warnings

The new campus culture of safe spaces and trigger warnings infantilizes students and prepares them to be unable to function in a world with diverse viewpoints.

> **Case: The Safe Space** (Chapter on Campus): "When a conservative speaker comes to campus, universities now offer 'safe spaces' with coloring books, Play-Doh, and therapy dogs for students who are 'traumatized' by hearing a viewpoint they disagree with. Meanwhile, conservative students are physically threatened and assaulted for expressing their views."
> **Key takeaway**: The left portrays itself as tolerant but cannot tolerate disagreement. Safe spaces are not about safety — they are about enforcing ideological conformity.

> **Case: The Trigger Warning** (Chapter on PC Culture): "Students now demand trigger warnings for classic books and historical lectures that might 'offend' them. College courses come with content warnings for everything. This does not protect students — it infantilizes them and prevents them from engaging with challenging ideas."
> **Key takeaway**: The culture of trigger warnings is creating a generation that cannot handle disagreement, adversity, or even uncomfortable ideas.
