# 3 — Campus and Generational Issues

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Indoctrination

Trump Jr. argues that universities have become left-wing indoctrination centers. Free speech is suppressed, conservative students are harassed, and education has been replaced with activism.

> **Case: The Safe Space** (Chapter on Campus): "Universities now offer 'safe spaces' with Play-Doh and therapy dogs when conservative speakers visit. Meanwhile, conservative students are threatened and assaulted for expressing their views."
> **Key takeaway**: The left protects its followers from ideas they disagree with. This is the opposite of education.

> **Case: The Trigger Warning** (Chapter on PC Culture): "Students demand trigger warnings for books and lectures that might 'offend' them. College courses now come with content warnings. This infantilizes young adults."
> **Key takeaway**: The culture of trigger warnings and safe spaces is creating a generation that cannot handle disagreement or adversity.
