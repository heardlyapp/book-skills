# 2 — Media Bias and Big Tech Censorship

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Media as Progressive Institution

Trump Jr. argues that mainstream media is not a neutral arbiter of truth — it is a progressive institution that consistently frames stories to support left-wing narratives and destroy conservative ones.

## Big Tech

Social media platforms have become the new public square. The companies that control them are run by progressives who actively suppress conservative speech.

> **Case: The Russia Story** (Chapter on Media): "For two years, the media dominated the news cycle with the Trump-Russia collusion story. Front-page headlines. Breaking news alerts. Hourly commentary. Then the Mueller report came out — no collusion. Did any of them apologize? No. They simply moved on to the next story." Trump Jr. argues this is a pattern.
> **Key takeaway**: Major news organizations will promote any narrative that damages conservatives, regardless of its truth. When proven false, they never issue corrections proportional to the original coverage.

> **Case: The Shadow Ban** (Chapter on Big Tech): "Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube systematically shadow-ban and deplatform conservative voices. They claim to be neutral platforms, but their algorithms suppress conservative content and their policies target conservative users." Trump Jr. presents this as a form of censorship.
> **Key takeaway**: The platforms that control the public square are not neutral. They use their power to shape political discourse in ways that favor the left.
