# 2 — Media Bias

> Source: "Triggered" by Donald Trump Jr.

## The Media Problem

Trump Jr. argues that mainstream media is not neutral. It consistently frames stories to support progressive narratives and destroy conservative ones.

> **Case: The Russia Story** (Chapter on Media): "For two years, the media told us that Trump colluded with Russia. They ran story after story. Then the Mueller report came out — no collusion. Did the media apologize? No. They moved on to the next story."
> **Key takeaway**: The media will promote any story that damages conservatives, regardless of its truth. They never apologize for being wrong.

> **Case: The Social Media Censorship** (Chapter on Big Tech): "Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube systematically shadow-ban and de-platform conservatives. They claim to be neutral platforms, but they are actively shaping political discourse."
> **Key takeaway**: Big tech controls the public square. They use that power to silence conservative voices.
