# Anti-Patterns — Medical Medium Cleanse to Heal

## The Central Error: "My Body Is Fine — I Don't Need to Cleanse"

"Living with symptoms has become the new normal." William argues that most people are sicker than they realize — they've just normalized their symptoms. "How many people do you know who struggle with their health? Chances are, most of the people in your life do." Case: The "hidden army" of millions who are "barely able to function" but appear fine in public.

### Mistake 1: "The body detoxes itself — cleanses are unnecessary"
"A rising belief in the health world is that we don't need to take special detox measures because the body does so naturally." William counters: the body's detox systems are overwhelmed by the unprecedented toxic burden of modern life. "If the body could handle everything on its own, chronic illness would not be at epidemic levels."

### Mistake 2: "All protein is good"
William specifically warns against: eggs (feed viruses), pork (high in toxins), and excessive protein powders. "The obsession with high protein has led people away from the healing power of fruits and vegetables." Case: "Eggs are a primary food source for viruses like Epstein-Barr."

### Mistake 3: "Intermittent fasting is good for everyone"
Chapter 5 is devoted to this. William argues that intermittent fasting can stress the adrenals and starve the liver of the glucose it needs for detoxification. "For people with chronic illness, intermittent fasting can be harmful."

### Mistake 4: "Fruit is too high in sugar"
"Fruit is not the enemy — the real enemy is fat combined with sugar." William encourages high fruit intake, arguing that natural fruit sugar (glucose + fructose) feeds the brain and liver while starving pathogens. Case: The 3:6:9 Cleanse includes dates, bananas, apples, and pears.

### Mistake 5: "Juicing removes the fiber — eat whole foods"
Chapter 6 addresses the juicing vs fiber debate. William argues both are important: "Juicing provides concentrated nutrients that the body can absorb instantly; fiber provides bulk for elimination." Both have their place. Case: Celery MUST be juiced, not blended, for the "cluster salts" to be effective.

### Mistake 6: "More protein and fat, fewer carbs"
"The high-protein, high-fat trend is keeping people sick." William's protocols are high fruit, high vegetable, low fat, and moderate protein. Case: "Fruit gives the liver the glucose it needs for the detox process. Without enough glucose, the liver cannot effectively neutralize and eliminate toxins."
