# Techniques — Models: Attract Women Through Honesty

## How to Attract Women Honestly (Based on Mark Manson)

### Technique 1: The Neediness Audit
Identify every area where you rely on women for your emotional state. "Neediness is: I need her to validate me. I need her to like me. I need her to approve of my choices." The technique: observe your own behavior. If you feel anxious waiting for a text back, that's neediness. If you change your opinion to agree with her, that's neediness. Case: Manson's post-breakup behavior — "I was desperate for validation and affection."

### Technique 2: The Polarization Frame
When you meet someone, don't try to charm them — try to reveal your true self as quickly as possible. Express your genuine interests and opinions, even if they might be unpopular. Case: "If you talk about how much you love heavy metal, you'll polarize women who hate heavy metal away — and the ones who love it will feel a stronger connection." The goal is not universal appeal — it's finding your tribe.

### Technique 3: The Vulnerable Statement
Practice saying things that feel uncomfortable to say. "I really like you." "I'd like to kiss you." "I'm nervous." Not as a pickup line — as genuine expression. Case: "Asking 'Can I kiss you?' is weak. Saying 'I want to kiss you' is vulnerable and powerful." Vulnerability means expressing desire without demanding reciprocation.

### Technique 4: The Three Fundamentals Checklist
Audit yourself on all three legs of the stool. (1) Demographics: where do you go, what do you offer? (2) Attractiveness: fashion, fitness, body language, vocal tonality. (3) Action: how often do you approach women? "If any of these is missing, you'll fall." Case: A man with great style (attractiveness) who never leaves his apartment (demographics) and doesn't approach (action) will get nowhere.

### Technique 5: The Honest Approach
Walk up to a woman and state your intentions directly. "Hi, I thought you were attractive and I wanted to come say hello." No pickup lines, no routines. "Creepiness is when a man's behavior doesn't match his intentions." Case: Manson's students who used openers like "I wanted to meet you" consistently got better reactions than students who used PUA scripts.

### Technique 6: The Pain Period Protocol
Accept that growth requires discomfort. Manson's advice: set a goal to get rejected 10 times. Each rejection is a rep — like lifting weights. Case: "Men who only experience rejection" — those who've never been rejected are the most afraid of it. Exposure therapy: the more you're rejected, the less it hurts.

### Technique 7: The Flirting Formula
"Demonstrate your interest while maintaining your frame." The balance: (1) Make your intention clear (I find you attractive). (2) Don't seek her permission to feel that way. (3) Let her come to you. Case: "I'm interested in getting to know you" + silence (let her respond). This expresses interest without neediness.
