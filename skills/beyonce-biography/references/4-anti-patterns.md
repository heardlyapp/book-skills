# 4 — Philanthropy and Giving Back

> Source: "Beyoncé: A Little Golden Book Biography" by Lavaille Lavette

## BeyGOOD Foundation

Created in 2013 to help people who don't have enough to eat and to provide homes for people without shelter. The mission is to inspire kindness, charity, and being good to yourself, others, and the world.

> **Case: Sharing Success** (2013): "Beyoncé wanted to share her success with others." She created a foundation to help people. Success is not just about what you achieve — it's about what you do with it.
> **Key takeaway**: The most successful people use their success to help others. Find a way to give back, no matter how small.
