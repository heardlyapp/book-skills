# 1 — The Daily Devotional Framework

> Source: "Live in Grace, Walk in Love" by Bob Goff

## Structure of Each Day

Each of the 365 devotions follows a consistent structure:
1. A short story or observation from Goff's life
2. A spiritual insight drawn from the story
3. A Scripture reference
4. A practical takeaway / action step

## Grace as the Foundation

Every devotion starts from the premise: you are loved. Not because of what you did, but because of who God is.

> **Case: The Everyday Stories** (Throughout): Goff draws spiritual lessons from ordinary moments — a conversation with a friend, a walk on the beach, a meal with his family. "God is in the everyday." The devotional format makes this explicit: every day has potential for divine encounter.
> **Key takeaway**: You don't need a mountaintop experience to meet God. He's in the ordinary. Open your eyes to today.

> **Case: The Action Step** (Each Devotion): Every day ends with something you can DO. Goff is relentless about this: love is a verb. Reading is not enough. You must act.
> **Key takeaway**: A devotional that doesn't lead to action is incomplete. The point is not knowledge — it's transformation.
