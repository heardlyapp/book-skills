# 2 — Understanding Grace

> Source: "Live in Grace, Walk in Love" by Bob Goff

## Grace Is Unearned

The most important thing about grace: you can't earn it. You can't lose it. You can only receive it. This is liberating for people who have spent their lives trying to be good enough.

## Grace Every Day

Grace is not a one-time event. You need it every day. Just like food, water, oxygen. Grace is the air of the spiritual life.

> **Case: The Performance Trap** (Early Devotions): Goff addresses the people who think they need to "get right" before approaching God. His response: you can't get right. That's why you need grace. Come as you are. Grace will meet you there.
> **Key takeaway**: Stop trying to earn what's already been given. You can't make God love you more. You can't make Him love you less.

> **Case: The Second Chance** (Mid-Year Devotions): Goff tells the story of someone who messed up badly and thought they were disqualified. Grace says: you're not disqualified. You're invited back. Not because of what you did, but because of who God is.
> **Key takeaway**: Grace doesn't mean there are no consequences. It means there's always a way back. Always.
