## P/PC Balance (Production / Production Capability)

**The Goose and the Golden Egg:** A farmer discovers a goose that lays golden eggs (P = Production). He gets rich, but grows impatient and kills the goose to get all the eggs at once — and gets nothing (PC = Production Capability destroyed).

**Principle:** Effectiveness = Balance between P and PC. Results matter, but so does preserving the capacity to produce results.

**Application:**
- At work: meet deadlines (P) while training your team (PC)
- In relationships: get tasks done (P) while nurturing trust (PC)
- With yourself: achieve goals (P) while exercising and resting (PC)

## The Emotional Bank Account (EBA)

Trust in a relationship is like a bank account. You make deposits (kindness, honesty, keeping promises) and withdrawals (rudeness, disrespect, breaking trust).

**Major Deposits:**
1. Understand the individual — what's a deposit for one may be a withdrawal for another
2. Attend to little things — small kindnesses add up
3. Keep commitments
4. Clarify expectations — unmet expectations = withdrawals
5. Show personal integrity
6. Apologize sincerely when you make a withdrawal

**When the balance is high:** communication is easy, mistakes are forgiven, differences are valued.
**When the balance is low:** every interaction is tense, you have to watch every word.
