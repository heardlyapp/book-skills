# 4 — Media Coverage

> Source: "The Russia Hoax" by Gregg Jarrett

## The Media Role

Jarrett argues the media played a significant role in promoting the collusion narrative. Key news organizations reported extensively on the investigation, often with uncritical acceptance of government leaks.

> **Case: The CNN Retraction** (Chapter on Media): CNN reported in 2017 that the Trump campaign had colluded with Russia based on "congressional sources." The story was later retracted. Three CNN journalists resigned. "The retraction was buried. The original story had been front-page news."
> **Key takeaway**: Major news outlets reported unverified claims about collusion. When those claims were disproven, the corrections received far less attention than the original stories. This asymmetry shaped public perception.
