# 3 — The Mueller Report

> Source: "The Russia Hoax" by Gregg Jarrett

## The Investigation

Robert Mueller was appointed Special Counsel in May 2017. The investigation lasted 22 months. Result: no finding of criminal conspiracy.

## Key Findings

- Russia interfered in the 2016 election (social media, hacking).
- No criminal conspiracy between the Trump campaign and Russia.
- No conclusion on obstruction of justice (Mueller did not reach a traditional prosecutorial judgment).

> **Case: The Obstruction Analysis** (Chapter 9): Mueller did not recommend charges for obstruction, but also did not exonerate Trump. "If we had confidence that the President clearly did not commit a crime, we would say so." This unique formulation left the issue unresolved.
> **Key takeaway**: Mueller's decision not to reach a conclusion on obstruction was unusual. It led to conflicting interpretations: one side said "no crime," the other said "not exonerated."
