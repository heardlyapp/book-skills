# Common Mistakes — Anti-Patterns

**Anti-Pattern 1: False Equivalence. Not all political crises are the same. Democratic backsliding is real and must be distinguished from normal politics.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: Constitutional Faith. Believing that written rules alone will protect democracy. Norms, not text, are what matter.**

**Case: The Weimar Constitution. Germany had a strong constitution. It did not prevent Hitler.**

**Case: The Electoral Process. Elections alone do not protect democracy. Authoritarians can win elections.**

**Case: The Courts. Courts can be packed. Judicial independence is fragile.**

**Case: The Media. A free press can be undermined. Authoritarians attack journalists.**
