# Democratic Backsliding — Core Framework

Levitsky and Ziblatt argue that modern democracies die by gradualism, not by coup.

**Case: Germany 1933. Hitler was appointed chancellor legally. He used the Reichstag Fire to suspend civil liberties. Democracy died within months — all through legal means.**

**Case: Peru 1992. Fujimori dissolved Congress and suspended the constitution. The coup was popular. Democracy died by popular demand.**

**Case: Venezuela 1999. Chávez was elected democratically. He then rewrote the constitution, packed the courts, and silenced the opposition.**

**Case: Italy 1922. Mussolini marched on Rome. The king appointed him prime minister. Democracy surrendered without a fight.**

**Case: The Enabling Act. Hitler passed a law giving himself dictatorial powers. It was constitutional. The rule of law was used to destroy the rule of law.**
