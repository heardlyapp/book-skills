# Techniques: The 6-Week Action Steps

## Week 1: Credit Card Optimization
Pick 2-3 cards based on your spending. Set up automatic full-balance payments. Call to negotiate rates.

> **Case: The Auto-Pay Setup** (Chapter 1): A reader set up automatic credit card payments on payday. She never paid interest or late fees again. The credit card company even increased her credit limit automatically. The system worked without her thinking about it.
> **Key takeaway**: Automation is the key to credit card success. Set it once, and you'll never miss a payment again.

## Week 2: Beat the Banks
Open a high-yield online savings account. Open a free checking account. Close fee-charging accounts.

## Week 3: Get Ready to Invest
Open a Roth IRA. Choose a target-date fund. Set up automatic contributions.

## Week 4: Conscious Spending Plan
Divide after-tax income: 50-60% fixed, 10% guilt-free, 10-20% long-term savings, 5-10% short-term.

> **Case: The Ratio That Worked** (Chapter 4): A reader who made $60K used the Conscious Spending Plan to allocate $1,000/month to guilt-free spending. She stopped feeling deprived, stopped impulse-buying, and started saving $800/month automatically.
> **Key takeaway**: Including guilt-free spending in your plan is not a luxury — it's a necessity. Plans that don't allow for joy fail.

## Week 5: Automatic Investing
Set up automatic transfers on payday to investment accounts. Dollar-cost average.

## Week 6: The Rich Life
Define your Rich Life. Optimize earning. Celebrate your system.
