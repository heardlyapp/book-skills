# 4 — Quick Weeknight Fixes

> Source: "Fifty Shades of Chicken" by F.L. Fowler

## 5 Quick Meals (Under 30 Minutes)

1. **Pan-Seared Chicken Breast**: Hot cast iron, 5-7 mins skin-side down. Flip. Finish. Pan sauce.
2. **Chicken Thighs with Anything**: Thighs + salt + pepper + oven. 400°F, 25 mins. Add vegetables.
3. **Chicken Salad**: Leftover bird + mayo + herbs + celery + lemon. 10 minutes. Sandwiches or greens.
4. **Chicken Soup in a Hurry**: Boxed stock + leftover chicken + fresh veggies + egg noodles. 20 minutes.
5. **Sheet Pan Chicken**: Thighs + chopped vegetables. Toss with oil, salt. 400°F, 25 min. One pan.

> **Case: The Panic Dinner** (Chapter 4): "I don't know what I'm doing!" Ana panics as the Wolf watches. He calmly talks her through it: season, sear, don't move it. This is the universal beginner panic — and the universal fix: stop fidgeting. Let the pan do the work.
> **Key takeaway**: Most chicken-cooking mistakes come from not leaving the chicken alone. Let it cook. Let it rest. Don't poke, don't flip early, don't cut too soon.
