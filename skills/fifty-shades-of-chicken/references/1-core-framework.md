> Source: "Fifty Shades of Chicken" by F.L. Fowler

## 6 Core Cooking Methods

### 1. Roasting
Whole bird or bone-in parts. High heat (425°F). Season generously. Don't crowd the pan.

**Signature**: Classic Roast Chicken — "The Initiation." Brined, trussed, roasted to golden perfection.

### 2. Braising
Low and slow in liquid. Coq au vin, chicken cacciatore, chicken in white wine. Dark meat preferred.

**Signature**: Coq au Vin — "The French Lesson." Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, pearl onions, bacon.

### 3. Grilling
Direct high heat for parts, indirect for whole. Marinade overnight. Clean the grates.

### 4. Pan-Searing
Skin-side down in hot pan. Finish in oven. Make a pan sauce. 15 minutes total.

### 5. Frying
Buttermilk soak. Seasoned flour + cornstarch. 350°F oil. Internal temp 165°F.

### 6. Soups and Stews
Use the carcass for stock. Add aromatics. Simmer. Shred leftover meat. Noodles or rice.
