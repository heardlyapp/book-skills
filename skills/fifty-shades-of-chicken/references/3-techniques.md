# 3 — Showstopper Recipes

> Source: "Fifty Shades of Chicken" by F.L. Fowler

## Coq au Vin

The classic French braise. Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, pearl onions, and bacon.

## Roast Chicken with Lemon and Herbs

Simple, elegant, impressive. Brine overnight. Stuff cavity with lemon, garlic, thyme. Roast at 425°F.

## Chicken Roulade

Boneless thigh stuffed with cheese, herbs, or spinach. Rolled, tied, seared, finished in oven.

## Chicken in White Wine and Tarragon

Creamy, elegant. Bone-in thighs, white wine, cream, fresh tarragon. 30 minutes.
