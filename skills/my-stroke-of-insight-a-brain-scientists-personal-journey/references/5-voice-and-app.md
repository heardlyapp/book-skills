# Voice and Application: Taylor's Message

> Source: My Stroke of Insight, Chapters 15-20

## Taylor's Voice

Jill Bolte Taylor writes as both scientist and survivor. She is precise about anatomy but deeply personal about experience. Her central message: "We have the power to choose, moment by moment, who and how we want to be in the world."

## Application: Stroke Prevention and Recognition

Know the symptoms: sudden difficulty speaking, understanding, walking, seeing, or severe headache. Time is brain. Taylor's knowledge saved her life.

## Application: Recovery from Any Brain Injury

The principles apply broadly: create a peaceful environment, use simple language, repeat, don't test, celebrate small wins, get sleep.

## Application: Mental Health

Taylor argues that depression and anxiety are largely left-hemisphere phenomena — rumination, self-criticism, fear of the future. Accessing right-hemisphere nowness through meditation and presence can relieve suffering.

## Application: Finding Inner Peace

The right hemisphere's experience of nowness and connection is not mystical. It's normal brain function. We can cultivate it through practice — meditation, breathwork, nature, silence.

## Key Quotes

- "I am not the one who is angry. I am the one who is noticing that I am angry."
- "We have the power to choose, moment by moment, who and how we want to be in the world."
- "Nirvana is not a place you go — it's a brain state you can access."
- "Time is brain."
- "The left hemisphere tells a story. The right hemisphere experiences the truth."
