# 3 — Techniques

> Source: *Concise Guide to APA Style (7th ed.)* — Chapters 8-10.

## 5 Quick References

### 1. In-Text Citation

1 author: (Author, Year) or Author (Year) stated that...
2 authors: (Author & Author, Year)
3+ authors: (First Author et al., Year)
Direct quote: (Author, Year, p. X)
No author: use title in quotation marks

> **Example: 3+ Authors** (Chapter 8): For works with 3 or more authors, always use the first author's name plus "et al." in APA 7th. E.g., "Johnson et al. (2023) found that..." This is a change from 6th edition where you listed up to 5 authors the first time.

> **Example: Paraphrasing** (Chapter 8): When paraphrasing, include the author and year. Page numbers are recommended but not required unless quoting directly. "Theory of mind develops gradually in early childhood (Wellman, 2018)."

### 2. Journal Article Reference

Author. (Year). Article title. *Journal Title, Volume*(Issue), Pages. DOI

### 3. Book Reference

Author. (Year). *Book title*. Publisher. DOI

### 4. Website Reference

Author. (Year, Month Day). *Page title*. Site Name. URL

### 5. YouTube/Video

Uploader. (Year, Month Day). *Title of video* [Video]. Platform. URL
