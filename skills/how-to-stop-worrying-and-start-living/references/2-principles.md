# Principles of Worry Management

**Principle 1: Live in the Present. The past is gone. The future is not here. Only now exists.**

**Principle 2: Accept the Worst. Once you accept the worst outcome, fear disappears.**

**Principle 3: Stay Busy. Purposeful activity crowds out worry.**

**Case: The Businessman. A businessman was paralyzed by worry. He started working longer hours. His worry disappeared.**

**Case: The Doctor. A doctor prescribed activity for anxious patients. It worked better than medication.**

**Case: The Writer. Carnegie himself kept busy writing and teaching. It kept his own worries at bay.**
