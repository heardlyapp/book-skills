# Practical Anti-Worry Techniques

**Technique 1: Day-Tight Compartment. Imagine your life is a series of sealed compartments. Open only today's.**

**Technique 2: The Magic Formula. Write down the worst outcome. Accept it. Then work to improve on it.**

**Technique 3: Get the Facts. Write down the problem. Gather all facts. Most worries are based on incomplete information.**

**Case: The Insurance Salesman. He used the magic formula to overcome his fear of rejection.**

**Case: The Housewife. She used the day-tight compartment technique to stop worrying about her children.**

**Case: The Student. He analyzed his worry about exams and realized he was worrying about things he could control.**
