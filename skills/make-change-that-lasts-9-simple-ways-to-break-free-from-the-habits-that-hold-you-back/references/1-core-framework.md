# The Nine Reliances Framework

Chatterjee identifies nine hidden dependencies that block change.

**Case: The GP Patient. A patient expects the doctor to fix everything. Real change requires self-trust.**

**Case: The Perfectionist. A woman who cannot start until conditions are perfect. She never starts.**

**Case: The People-Pleaser. A man who cannot say no. His life is shaped by others' expectations.**

**Case: The Busy Professional. A woman who fills every moment with activity. She avoids stillness.**

**Case: The Giver. A patient who finds fulfillment by giving, not getting.**
