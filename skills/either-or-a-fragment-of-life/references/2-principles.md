# Principles of Existence

**Principle 1: Choose Yourself.** You are not born with a self. You become a self through choice. The person who never chooses never becomes an individual.

**Principle 2: The Leap Is Required.** The transition from one stage to another cannot be achieved through rational argument. It requires a passionate leap beyond reason.

**Principle 3: Anxiety Is Freedom.** Anxiety is not a defect. It is the recognition that you are free and responsible for your choices.

**Case: The Marriage Choice.** Judge William argues that marriage is not a rational calculation but a passionate commitment. You cannot calculate whether a marriage will succeed. You must leap.

**Case: The Aesthetic's Despair.** The aesthete avoids commitment. This avoidance is itself a choice. And it leads to despair because the person who never chooses never becomes a self.

**Case: The Ethical's Burden.** The ethical person struggles with responsibility. This struggle is the price of becoming a self.
