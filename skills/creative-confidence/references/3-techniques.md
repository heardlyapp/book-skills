# Techniques · Daily Creative Practice

> Source: *Creative Confidence*, Chapters 6-8

---

## Daily Practices

1. **Keep an idea journal** — Write down 3 ideas every day. No judgment. Just quantity.
2. **Do one thing differently** — Take a new route to work. Cook without a recipe. Break one routine.
3. **Prototype quickly** — When facing a problem, make one small physical or visual representation of a solution.

## Building Creative Confidence

Confidence comes from action, not thought. Take one small creative risk each day. Share an unfinished idea. Ask for feedback early.

## The 30-Second Rule

When someone shares a new idea, wait 30 seconds before responding. This prevents the automatic "that won't work" reflex and gives the idea space to breathe.
