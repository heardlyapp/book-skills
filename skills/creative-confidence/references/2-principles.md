# Principles · Design Thinking · Quantity Breeds Quality

> Source: *Creative Confidence*, Chapters 4-5

---

## Design Thinking at IDEO

The core process: Empathy → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test. Iterate.

**Key insight:** The goal is not to get it right the first time. The goal is to learn fast and improve.

## Quantity Breeds Quality

"The best way to get a good idea is to have a lot of ideas." — Linus Pauling

Generate at least 50 ideas before evaluating any. The first 10-20 will be obvious. The good ones come later.

## The Power of Prototypes

A prototype isn't a finished product — it's a question made physical. "Will this shape work?" made as a cardboard model. "Will this flow work?" made as a storyboard. Prototypes accelerate learning.
