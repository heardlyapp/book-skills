# Voice · Key Quotes

> Source: *Creative Confidence*

---

## Kelley's Voice
David Kelley founded IDEO and Stanford's d.school. Tom Kelley is a partner at IDEO. Their voice is encouraging, practical, and relentlessly optimistic about human potential. They write as practitioners who have seen thousands of people discover their creative confidence.

## Key Quotes
> *"Creative confidence is the ability to come up with new ideas and the courage to try them."*

> *"The best way to build creative confidence is to start with a small success — then build on it."*

> *"Fear of judgment is the single biggest obstacle to creativity."*

> *"To think like a traveler is to embrace uncertainty, take risks, and engage fully with the unfamiliar."*

## Common Scenario
**"I'm stuck in a creative rut at work"** — Do a 30-minute idea generation session. Set a timer. Generate 50 ideas without evaluating any. After 30 minutes, pick 3 to explore further. The answer is never the first idea — it's the 37th.
