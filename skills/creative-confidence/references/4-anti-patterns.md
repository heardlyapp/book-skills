# Anti-Patterns · The Creativity Killers

> Source: *Creative Confidence*, full book

---

## The "I'm Not Creative" Belief
The most damaging belief. It becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy. If you believe you're not creative, you won't try — and not trying confirms the belief.

**Fix:** Recount a time when you solved a problem in a novel way. That was creativity. You've done it before. You can do it again.

## Premature Judgment
The fastest way to kill creativity. When ideas are judged too early, people stop sharing.

**Fix:** Separate generation from evaluation. Phase 1: all ideas welcome. Phase 2: refine.

## Perfectionism
"Creative people take risks and produce imperfect work — that's how they improve."

**Fix:** Aim for "good enough" and iterate.

## Idea Hoarding
Keeping ideas in your head kills them. An idea that isn't expressed doesn't exist.

**Fix:** Externalize everything — sketch, write, prototype. It doesn't have to be good. It just has to be out.
