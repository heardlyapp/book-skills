# 5 — Adyashanti's Voice + Application

> Source: "The End of Your World" by Adyashanti

## Adyashanti's Voice

Adyashanti is a contemporary spiritual teacher from California. His voice: direct, uncompromising, compassionate. He has no interest in comforting you — he wants to wake you up.

## Key Quote

"Enlightenment is not the end of your problems. It's the end of your world."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Had an awakening and feel lost** → You're not lost. You're between worlds. The old self is gone. The new hasn't formed. Be patient.
2. **Trying to recreate a spiritual experience** → Stop. Experiences can't be recreated. Let them go. What remains is true.
3. **Feeling spiritually superior** → Check for spiritual ego. True awakening humbles.
4. **Using spirituality to avoid life** → Awakening doesn't take you away from life — it brings you fully into it.
5. **Wondering if you're "done"** → If you're asking, you're not. When you're done, you don't need to ask.
