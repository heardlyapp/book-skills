# Voice and Application: Vranich's Approach

> Source: Breathe, throughout

## Vranich's Voice

Dr. Belisa Vranich is a clinical psychologist who discovered that most health complaints — anxiety, fatigue, digestive issues, poor sleep — traced back to dysfunctional breathing. She writes as both a clinician and a coach: direct, encouraging, and relentlessly practical.

## Application: Anxiety Relief

The single most useful application. When anxious, the exhale is short. Extend it. 4 seconds in, 6 seconds out. This activates the vagus nerve and drops heart rate within 60 seconds.

## Application: Athletic Performance

Proper breathing improves core stability and oxygen delivery. Belly breathing during exercise is more efficient than chest breathing. Practice it on the treadmill first — low intensity, high reps.

## Application: Daily Health

Nose breathing during the day reduces stress. Mouth taping at night improves sleep. The book's 14-day program is designed to be integrated into life, not done as a separate practice.

## Application: Respiratory Conditions

The book profiles clients with asthma, COPD, and sleep apnea who improved significantly through breathing retraining. Breathing exercises complement medical treatment, not replace it.
