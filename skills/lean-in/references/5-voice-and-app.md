## Sandberg's Voice

Personal, vulnerable, data-backed. Sandberg shares her own mistakes and insecurities alongside research and statistics. She's not preaching from a position of perfection — she's sharing what she learned from getting it wrong.

> **Case:** At Facebook, Sandberg noticed that women were less likely to raise their hands for stretch assignments—roles where they didn't meet 100% of the qualifications. Men would apply with 60%. She started actively encouraging qualified women to take chances. The result: more women in leadership roles.
