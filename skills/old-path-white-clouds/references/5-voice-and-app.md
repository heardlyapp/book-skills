# 5 — Nhat Hanh's Voice + Application

> Source: "Old Path White Clouds" by Thich Nhat Hanh

## Nhat Hanh's Voice

Thich Nhat Hanh (1926-2022) was a Vietnamese Buddhist monk, peace activist, and one of the most influential teachers of mindfulness in the West. His voice: gentle, clear, poetic. He writes like he speaks — with the calm authority of someone who has practiced what he teaches for 80 years.

## Key Quote

"Walk as if you are kissing the earth with your feet."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Overwhelmed by anxiety** → Breathe. One conscious breath. The breath is always with you.
2. **Grieving a loss** → Impermanence is not a tragedy — it's a fact. The pain is real but so is the love that continues.
3. **Stuck in anger** → Anger is a fire. Don't feed it. Breathe, walk, touch the earth. The fire will cool.
4. **Feeling disconnected** → Interbeing: you are not separate. You are connected to everyone and everything.
5. **Wanting to meditate but don't know how** → Start with one breath. Then one step. Then one tangerine segment.
