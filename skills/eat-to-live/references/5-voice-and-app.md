## Key Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Starting the Diet
Commit to 6 weeks. Stock your kitchen with G-BOMBS. Prepare for 3-5 days of withdrawal. Eat large salads before every meal.

### Scenario 2: Eating Out
Order a large salad with beans or grilled vegetables. Ask for dressing on the side (use lemon or vinegar). Avoid oil, cheese, and processed ingredients.

### Scenario 3: Sugar Addiction
The first 3-5 days are hardest. Eat fruit when cravings hit. The sweetness of fruit satisfies without perpetuating the addiction.

### Scenario 4: Family Meals
Cook one meal everyone can eat — a large salad, bean soup, roasted vegetables. Add a side of protein/grain for family members not ready to go all-in.

### Scenario 5: Travel
Pack nuts, seeds, dried fruit, and instant soup packets. Find grocery stores at your destination. Salad bars are your best option.
