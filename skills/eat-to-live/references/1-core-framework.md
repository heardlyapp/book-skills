# Core Framework: Nutrient Density and the Nutritarian Approach

## The Central Equation

Dr. Fuhrman's entire framework rests on one equation: **Health = Nutrients / Calories**. The foods with the highest nutrient density promote health. Foods with low nutrient density (regardless of calories) damage health. Weight loss and disease reversal are natural outcomes of eating high-nutrient foods.

**The ANDI Score**: The Aggregate Nutrient Density Index ranks foods on a scale of 1 to 1000 based on nutrients per calorie. Kale scores 1000. Collard greens 1000. Soda scores 1.

> **Case: The Salad as Medicine** (Chapter 2): Dr. Fuhrman describes patients with chronic diseases—heart disease, diabetes, autoimmune conditions—who reversed their conditions by adopting a high-nutrient diet. One patient with severe coronary artery disease avoided bypass surgery by switching to the Nutritarian diet. His follow-up scans showed dramatically reduced arterial plaque.
> **Key takeaway**: The body has remarkable healing capacity when given the right nutritional building blocks. Food is not just fuel—it's medicine.

## G-BOMBS: The Superfood Framework

| Food Group | Why | How to Eat |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| **G**reens | Highest ANDI scores on the planet | Large salad before lunch and dinner |
| **B**eans | Fiber, protein, resistant starch | Daily — soups, stews, salads |
| **O**nions | Organosulfur compounds fight cancer | Raw or cooked, daily |
| **M**ushrooms | Immune-supporting compounds | 1+ servings daily |
| **B**erries | Highest antioxidant content | 1+ servings daily (frozen is fine) |
| **S**eeds | Healthy fats and minerals | Flax, chia, hemp, sunflower |

> **Case: The Toxic Hunger Theory** (Chapter 3): Dr. Fuhrman explains that the discomfort people feel when they stop eating processed food is not true hunger—it's withdrawal. Processed foods, sugar, and salt create addictive-like responses. The irritability, cravings, and anxiety people experience in the first few days of healthy eating are "toxic hunger"—elimination symptoms—not genuine physical need.
> **Key takeaway**: Don't give in to the first 3-5 days. Toxic hunger passes. True hunger is gentle and doesn't demand immediate gratification.
