# Principles of Leadership Language

**Principle 1: Separate Redwork from Bluework. Do not mix execution and thinking.**

**Principle 2: Control the Clock, Not the Work. Set boundaries, trust the team.**

**Principle 3: Commit Before Act. Use "I intend to" to create ownership.**

**Case: The Navy Nuclear Power. Traditional command culture is the opposite of Marquet's approach.**

**Case: The Crew Empowerment. Crew members who were told what to do became passive. Those who stated intentions became engaged.**
