# 5 — Martin's Voice + Application

> Source: "Heaven: Think On These Things" by Carl Martin

## Martin's Voice

Carl Martin is a Hollywood filmmaker and screenwriter who wrote a book on heaven. His voice: imaginative, warm, accessible, biblically grounded. He uses his filmmaker's eye to paint vivid pictures of what heaven might be like.

## Key Quote

> "The subject of heaven is central to the believer's deepest longing."

## 5 Application Scenarios

1. **Grieving the loss of a loved one** → Heaven is real. The separation is temporary. You will see them again.
2. **Fear of death** → Death is not the end — it's a doorway. For the believer, to be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord.
3. **Feeling heaven is distant or irrelevant** → "Think on these things." Meditating on heaven changes your perspective on everything else.
4. **Doubting heaven's reality** → Martin's book is an invitation to explore the biblical evidence. Ask honest questions. Let Scripture speak.
5. **Living without eternal perspective** → Your choices today matter for eternity. Live now in light of then.
