# 5 — Cheney's Voice + Application

> Source: "Oath and Honor" by Liz Cheney

## Cheney's Voice

Liz Cheney is a conservative Republican from Wyoming, daughter of former Vice President Dick Cheney. Her voice: serious, principled, understated. She doesn't grandstand. She states facts and lets them speak.

## Key Quote

"Our oath to the Constitution is not conditional. It does not depend on our party winning. It does not depend on our political survival. It is absolute."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Pressured to stay silent about wrongdoing** → Cheney's example: silence is complicity. Speak.
2. **Confused about what happened on Jan 6** → Read the committee report. Watch the hearings. The evidence is public.
3. **Worried about democracy** → Cheney is too. But hope is not passive. Defending democracy requires action.
4. **Disagree with your party's direction** → You don't have to leave. But you don't have to go along. Stand on principle.
5. **Feeling helpless as an individual** → Vote in every election. Pay attention. Hold officials accountable. Democracy depends on citizens.
