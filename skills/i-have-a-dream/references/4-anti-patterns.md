# Anti-Patterns — Misreadings of the Speech

**Anti-Pattern 1: Colorblindness Without Justice. The dream is not about ignoring race. It is about justice making judgment by character possible.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: The Dream as Passive Hope. King's dream is active. It demands struggle.**

**Case: The Mistake of Gradualism. King rejected those who said wait. Waiting is not patience. It is complicity.**

**Case: The Misuse of Character. "Content of character" is weaponized against affirmative action. King supported affirmative action.**

**Case: The Dream as Fantasy. The dream is not a fantasy. It is a demand on the nation.**

**Case: The Timelessness. Some believe the speech is only about 1963. Its vision is unfinished.**
