# Practical Applications — Techniques

## Technique 1: Maintain Frame

The most important technique. Frame is the psychological context of any interaction. Whoever controls the frame controls the dynamic.

**How to do it:** When someone tries to pull you into their emotional frame — tears, anger, guilt trips, silent treatment — do not react. Acknowledge their emotion without adopting it. "I can see you're upset. We can talk about this when you're calm." Then disengage. Let them come to you.

**Case: The Emotional Accusation.** A woman says: "You never listen to me. You don't care about my feelings." The AFC response: defensive explanations, promises to do better. The rational male response: maintain frame. "I hear you. I disagree. If you want to discuss this calmly, I'm here." No defensiveness. No emotional reaction.

**Case: The Threat to Leave.** "Maybe we should just break up." The rational male does not panic. He does not beg. He says: "If that's what you want, I respect your decision." The willingness to walk away is the most powerful frame-maintainer.

## Technique 2: Cultivate Abundance

Abundance is not about having sex with many women. It is about internalizing the knowledge that you do not NEED any particular woman. You CHOOSE her.

**How to do it:** Keep living your life regardless of relationship status. Maintain your hobbies, your friendships, your career direction, your physical fitness. Never make a woman the center of your universe. When you have abundance, you do not accept behavior that violates your standards.

**Case: The Canceled Date.** A woman cancels a date at the last minute. The scarcity-minded man becomes anxious, wonders what he did wrong, reschedules immediately. The abundance-minded man says: "No problem, let me know when you're free." He genuinely does not care because he has other options.

**Case: The Text Game.** A man who texts a woman and gets no response should not double-text, triple-text, or ask "what's wrong?" A man with abundance moves on. If she responds later, great. If not, there are others.

## Technique 3: The Willingness to Walk Away

Genuine indifference to loss is the most powerful dynamic in any relationship. When a woman knows you will leave rather than accept mistreatment, she will not test you.

**How to do it:** Set clear boundaries. Enforce them consistently. If a woman violates a boundary, tell her once. If she does it again, walk away. Do not threaten to walk away — walk away. She will either come back with a different attitude, or she was not worth keeping.

**Case: The Boundary Test.** A woman screams at a man during an argument. He tells her: "I will not be spoken to that way. If you can't speak to me respectfully, I'm leaving." She tests him by screaming again. He leaves. She learns the boundary is real.

**Case: The Disrespect in Public.** A woman insults her partner in front of friends. He does not react in the moment — that would be frame loss. Later, privately, he tells her: "That was not acceptable. If it happens again, we're done."

## Technique 4: Lead, Do Not Follow

Women are attracted to men who lead. Leadership means making decisions, setting direction, and taking responsibility for outcomes.

**How to do it:** Make the plans. Decide the restaurant. Choose where to sit. Lead the conversation. Do not ask "What do you want to do?" Make a suggestion and see if she follows. Leadership is attractive. Indecision is not.

**Case: The First Date.** The rational male does not ask "Where do you want to go?" He says "I'm picking you up at 7. We're going to this Italian place on Elm Street. Wear something nice." He leads. She follows.

**Case: The Relationship Direction.** In a long-term relationship, the rational male sets the direction. He knows what he wants — marriage, children, lifestyle — and he leads toward it. He does not wait for her to define the relationship.

## Technique 5: Understand the Iron Rules

The Iron Rules of Tomassi are 12 non-negotiable principles. Memorize them.

**Selected Rules:**
- Rule 1: Frame is everything. Never abandon your frame for a woman's emotions.
- Rule 2: Never enter a relationship with a woman who values her orbiters over you.
- Rule 5: All women have an "alpha widow" — a past lover who set the standard she compares all men to.
- Rule 9: The quality of a woman's attachment to you is proportional to her perception of your abundance of options.
- Rule 10: What you tolerate, you encourage. Set boundaries and enforce them.

**Case: Rule 9 in Practice.** A man who is desperate for a relationship signals low SMV. The woman perceives he has no options and behaves accordingly — she becomes less invested. A man who has abundance signals high SMV. She becomes more invested. Rule 9 is counter-intuitive: the less you need her, the more she wants you.

**Case: Rule 10 in Action.** A man tolerates a woman canceling plans repeatedly. He does not enforce the boundary. She learns that cancellations have no consequences. Rule 10: what you tolerate, you encourage.
