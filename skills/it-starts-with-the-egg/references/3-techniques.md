# Techniques — It Starts with the Egg

## How to Improve Egg Quality (Based on Rebecca Fett)

### Technique 1: The Toxin Audit
Replace all plastic food containers with glass. Avoid canned foods (BPA lining). Refuse thermal receipt paper. Use unscented cleaning and personal care products (phthalate-free). Check recycling codes: avoid #3 (phthalates), #7 (BPA). Case: Fett's own "pre-pregnant" approach — she protected her eggs the way she would protect a growing baby. Eliminating plastics was her first step.

### Technique 2: The CoQ10 Protocol
Take ubiquinol (active CoQ10) 200-600 mg daily, divided into morning and evening doses. Start 3 months before attempting conception or IVF. CoQ10 powers the mitochondria that drive egg maturation. Case: Studies show CoQ10 improves embryo quality and reduces aneuploidy. Fett's remarkable IVF result (all 19 eggs becoming blastocysts) suggests CoQ10 played a key role.

### Technique 3: The Personalized Supplement Plan
Identify your specific fertility issue, then select supplements:
- Diminished ovarian reserve → DHEA (25-75 mg daily for 3 months before IVF)
- PCOS → Myo-inositol (4 g daily)
- Any IVF → CoQ10 + Melatonin (3 mg at night, but only for IVF cycle, not natural conception)
- General egg quality → NAC (600 mg), vitamin C, vitamin E
Case: Fett was diagnosed with diminished ovarian reserve and used DHEA + CoQ10. The ultrasound showed "about 20 eggs maturing" — normalization from a diminished reserve.

### Technique 4: The Egg Quality Diet
Cut refined carbohydrates. Eat a Mediterranean diet with extra emphasis on antioxidants: berries, dark leafy greens, avocado, olive oil, nuts. Reduce caffeine to one cup or less. Avoid trans fats completely. "The egg quality diet is fundamentally about stabilizing blood sugar and providing antioxidant protection."

### Technique 5: The "Pre-Pregnant" Mindset
Fett's approach: "During those months, I started thinking of myself as 'pre-pregnant' and protected my eggs the way I would protect a growing baby if I were pregnant." Any exposure or behavior you wouldn't allow during pregnancy (smoking, alcohol, toxins, poor diet) should be avoided during the 3 months before conception.

### Technique 6: The Sperm Quality Parallel
Male partners should also take CoQ10 (200-300 mg), zinc (30 mg), selenium (200 mcg), vitamin C (500 mg). Avoid hot baths, saunas, tight underwear. Sperm quality improves in 2-3 months. Case: "Sperm quality can be dramatically improved with simple changes — many men see significant improvement within one sperm cycle (about 72 days)."

### Technique 7: The Research Audit
Fett's method: don't trust internet fertility forums. Look for studies confirmed by multiple research groups involving real patients — not isolated animal studies. Check the references at the back of the book. "Individual studies, and particularly animal or test-tube studies, provide only limited evidence and must be taken with a grain of salt."
