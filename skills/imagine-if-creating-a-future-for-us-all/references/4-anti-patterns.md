# Anti-Patterns — What Stifles Creativity

**Anti-Pattern 1: Standardization. Treating all students the same ignores their unique talents.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: Fear of Being Wrong. Schools punish mistakes. Creativity requires risk-taking.**

**Case: The Exam System. Standardized tests measure only a narrow band of ability.**

**Case: The Curriculum. A fixed curriculum leaves no room for student interests or passions.**

**Case: The Hierarchy. Academic subjects are ranked above arts and vocational subjects.**

**Case: The Compliance. Schools reward compliance, not creativity. Following instructions is valued over independent thinking.**

**Case: The Test Scores. Countries that rank highest in test scores do not produce the most innovative graduates.**
