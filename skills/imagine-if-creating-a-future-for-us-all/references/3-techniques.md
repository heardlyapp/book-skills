# Techniques for Cultivating Creativity

**Technique 1: Find Your Element. Discover what you love and are good at. Then pursue it.**

**Technique 2: Embrace Divergent Thinking. There are many answers to every question. Explore them.**

**Technique 3: Reframe Failure. Failure is not the opposite of success. It is part of the creative process.**
