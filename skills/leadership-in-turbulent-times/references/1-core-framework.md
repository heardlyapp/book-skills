# Four Presidents, Four Crises

**Case: FDR's Polio. Polio paralyzed FDR's legs but freed his spirit. He led with optimism.**

**Case: LBJ's Poverty. Johnson's experience of poverty drove his passion for the Great Society.**

**Case: The Civil War. Lincoln preserved the Union through four years of brutal war.**
