# Voice and Application: Kaufman's Approach

> Source: The Personal MBA, throughout

## Kaufman's Voice

Josh Kaufman writes as a teacher and researcher — he spent years reading business books and synthesizing the most important concepts. His voice is clear, systematic, and accessible. He believes business knowledge should be free and available to anyone willing to read and practice.

## Application: Starting a Business

Before you launch, ask: What problem am I solving for someone? Who has this problem? Will they pay for a solution? The Iron Law of the Market: if you're not solving a real problem, you don't have a business.

## Application: Growing a Business

Check the 5 parts systematically: value creation, marketing, sales, value delivery, finance. The weakest part determines your growth ceiling. Improve that one first.

## Application: Improving Operations

Use the 3-System model. Identify which system (create demand, fulfill demand, support demand) is breaking down. Fix that system before optimizing the others.

## Application: Personal Productivity

The Fogg Behavior Model applies to personal habits too. To build any new habit: increase your motivation, make the behavior extremely easy, and create consistent triggers.
