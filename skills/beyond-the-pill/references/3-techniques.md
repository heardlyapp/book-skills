# Techniques — Beyond the Pill

## How to Balance Hormones After the Pill (Based on Dr. Jolene Brighten)

### Technique 1: The Hormone Quiz — Find Your Imbalance First
Before starting any protocol, Brighten provides a comprehensive hormone quiz with 8 categories: estrogen excess (A), progesterone deficiency (B), PMS/PMDD (C), androgen excess/PCOS (D), low testosterone (E), adrenal fatigue (F), cortisol imbalance (G), and hypothyroidism (H). Total your scores in each category to identify your primary imbalance. Case example: a woman scoring high in Category A (bloating, heavy periods, mood swings) would focus on estrogen detox through cruciferous vegetables and DIM.

### Technique 2: The Liver-Emptying Morning Routine
Support liver detox first thing: warm lemon water, followed by a green smoothie with cruciferous greens. Brighten's Creamy Strawberry Cauliflower Smoothie is a key recipe — cauliflower provides sulfur for liver detox while berries offer antioxidants. This routine supports the liver's natural detoxification of synthetic hormones.

### Technique 3: The Gut Repair Protocol
The pill increases intestinal permeability (leaky gut) and alters the microbiome. Brighten's protocol: eliminate dairy, gluten, and processed foods for 30 days. Introduce bone broth (collagen for gut lining), L-glutamine (repairs intestinal cells), and probiotics (restore microbiome diversity). Case: Angela had gut issues from 13 years on the pill — her period returned after following this protocol for 30 days.

### Technique 4: Nutrient Repletion After the Pill
The pill depletes specific nutrients: folate, B12, B6, magnesium, zinc, selenium, and CoQ10. Brighten provides a supplement plan: magnesium glycinate for sleep/mood, zinc picolinate for skin/hormones, methylated B complex for energy/mood, vitamin C for adrenal support, vitamin D3 + K2 for immune function.

### Technique 5: Blood Sugar Balancing
The pill causes insulin resistance. Brighten's metabolic protocol: eat protein with every meal, avoid eating carbs alone, eat within one hour of waking, eat every 3-4 hours, finish eating 3 hours before bed. Case: Brighten herself gained 10 pounds on the pill that wouldn't come off until she balanced blood sugar through this protocol.

### Technique 6: The Fertility Awareness Method (FAM)
Brighten provides detailed guidance on FAM as a non-hormonal birth control alternative: track basal body temperature, cervical mucus, and cervical position daily. "With perfect use, the fertility awareness method is 99% effective." She includes specific guidance on charting apps, thermometer types, and when to avoid unprotected sex.

### Technique 7: Adaptogenic Stress Support
The pill taxes the adrenals. Brighten recommends adaptogens: ashwagandha (cortisol reduction), rhodiola (energy), holy basil (stress resilience), and maca (libido + hormone balance). Her Maca Latte recipe is a daily protocol for adrenal and libido support.
