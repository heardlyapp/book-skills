# 5 — Gaskin's Voice + Application

> Source: "Spiritual Midwifery" by Ina May Gaskin

## Gaskin's Voice

Ina May Gaskin is known as the "mother of modern midwifery." She co-founded The Farm Midwifery Center in Tennessee in 1971, which has delivered over 3,000 babies with outcomes rivaling or exceeding hospital births.

## Key Quote

"Birth is not a medical event. It is a profound, spiritual, life-changing experience that women have been having since the beginning of time."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Planning a natural birth** → Start with education. Read positive stories. Find a supportive midwife.
2. **Fear of childbirth** → The fear-tension-pain cycle. Relaxation is the answer. Practice now.
3. **In late pregnancy** → Squat daily. Eat protein. Rest. Your body is preparing.
4. **In labor** → Move. Vocalize low. Use water. Trust your body.
5. **Postpartum** → Rest. Breastfeed. Accept help. Your body did an amazing thing.
