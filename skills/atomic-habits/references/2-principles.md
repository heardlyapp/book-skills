# 关键原则 · 环境设计 · 习惯堆叠 · 两分钟法则 · 最小努力

> 源自《原子习惯》第 5-6、12、15 章

---

## 环境比意志力更重要

> 第 6 章 "Motivation Is Overrated; Environment Often Matters More"

原文: *"People with better self-control are better at structuring their lives so that they don't have to demonstrate herculean willpower. In other words, they spend less time in tempting situations."*

**核心: 每个习惯都由某个提示启动。让提示更明显，习惯就更自动。**

**原文案例: 医院手部清洁 (第 6 章)**
> 研究发现医生护士都知道洗手的重要性，但执行率很低。问题不是"他们不重视"，而是洗手池不在他们常规动线上。**把洗手液放在必经之处后，执行率飙升。**

### 一个空间, 一个用途
原文: *"When you walk into your room, you see a bed. Thus, sleeping is the habit that most strongly correlates with your bedroom. If you want a new habit to stick, it helps to have a consistent context."*

## 习惯堆叠 (Habit Stacking)

> 第 5 章 "The Best Way to Start a New Habit"

原文: *"One of the best ways to build a new habit is to identify a current habit you already do each day and then stack your new behavior on top. This is called habit stacking."*

公式:
> **"在[当前习惯]之后，我将[新习惯]"**

原文案例 (第 5 章): 作者自己的习惯堆叠序列:
> "在倒完晨间咖啡后，我会冥想一分钟。在冥想一分钟之后，我会写下当天要做的三件事。"

---

## 两分钟法则 (The Two-Minute Rule)

> 第 13 章 "How to Stop Procrastinating by Using the Two-Minute Rule"

原文: *"When you start a new habit, it should take less than two minutes to do. ... The idea is to make your habits as easy as possible to start. ... You can't improve a habit that doesn't exist. The two-minute rule is about mastering the habit of showing up."*

| 你想养成的习惯 | 两分钟版本 |
|--------------|-----------|
| 每晚读 1 小时 | 读 1 页书 |
| 每天跑 5 公里 | 穿上跑鞋 |
| 写 1000 字日记 | 写一句话 |
| 冥想 30 分钟 | 深呼吸三次 |

> **核心**: 先"标准化"，再"优化"。"读一页"优于"读一小时"——因为一页无法拒绝，拒绝只需要翻开。

---

## 最小努力法则 (Law of Least Effort)

> 第 12 章 "The Law of Least Effort"

原文: *"Human behavior follows the Law of Least Effort. We will naturally gravitate toward the option that requires the least amount of work."*

**设计策略:**
- **减摩擦**: 让好习惯容易做。健身服摆好、电脑打开到要写的页面。
- **增摩擦**: 让坏习惯难做。把电视遥控器电池拿走、手机锁在另一个房间。

---

## 即时奖赏 (The Cardinal Rule of Behavior Change)

> 第 15 章 "The Cardinal Rule of Behavior Change"

原文: *"What is immediately rewarded is repeated. What is immediately punished is rarely repeated. That's why the Cardinal Rule of Behavior Change is: what is immediately rewarded is repeated. What is immediately punished is avoided."*

**好习惯的奖赏是延迟的（运动不会立刻瘦，学习不会立刻涨薪），所以必须设计立即奖赏来连接行为和反馈。**
