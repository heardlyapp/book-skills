# 3 — Practical Parenting Techniques

> Source: "Dear Ijeawele" by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

## 5 Techniques

1. **Read widely.** What she reads shapes what she imagines is possible.
2. **Use inclusive language.** "Firefighter" not "fireman." "Humanity" not "mankind."
3. **Share household work.** Sons should learn to cook and clean. Daughters should learn tools.
4. **Let her choose.** Clothes, toys, interests — don't gender-code them.
5. **Teach her to question authority.** "Why should I?" is a valid question.
