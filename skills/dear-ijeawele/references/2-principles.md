# 2 — Principles of Feminist Parenting

> Source: "Dear Ijeawele" by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

## 5 Core Principles

1. **Equality is modeled, not taught.** Your child learns equality by seeing it at home.
2. **Language creates reality.** What you say shapes what she believes is possible.
3. **Curiosity over compliance.** A questioning child is stronger than an obedient one.
4. **Culture is not static.** "We've always done it this way" is not a good reason.
5. **Financial freedom is freedom.** Teach her to earn, save, and control her money.
