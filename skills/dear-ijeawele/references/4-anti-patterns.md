# 4 — Anti-Patterns: What to Avoid

> Source: "Dear Ijeawele" by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

## 5 Traps

1. **The Likeability Trap** — Teaching her to be nice, agreeable, pleasing. The most dangerous word: "ladylike."
2. **The Marriage Trap** — Implying her ultimate goal is marriage. "When you get married someday."
3. **The Tradition Trap** — "It's our culture" used to justify inequality.
4. **The Protection Trap** — Over-protecting her from the world instead of preparing her for it.
5. **The Comparison Trap** — Comparing her to other girls. Teach her she is enough.
