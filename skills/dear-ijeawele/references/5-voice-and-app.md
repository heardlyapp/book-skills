# 5 — Adichie's Voice + Application

> Source: "Dear Ijeawele" by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

## Voice

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie is one of the most celebrated contemporary writers. Her voice: precise, warm, unflinching. This book began as a Facebook post — it's the most accessible and personal of her works.

## Key Quote

> "Culture does not make people. People make culture. If it is true that the full humanity of women is not our culture, then we can and must make it our culture."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Daughter told to "be ladylike"** → Reject the framing. Ask: "Would you say that to a boy?"
2. **Family pressures about traditional roles** → Culture can be remade. Choose what serves your child.
3. **Daughter worried about being liked** → Likeability is not a virtue. Kindness is.
4. **Gatekeeping gender norms at school** → Question everything. Teach your daughter to question too.
5. **Raising a son who is a feminist** → The same principles apply. Boys need feminism too.
