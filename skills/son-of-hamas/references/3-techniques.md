# Techniques — Son of Hamas

## How a Hamas Prince Became an Israeli Spy

### Technique 1: The Trojan Horse
As the son of a Hamas founder, Mosab had natural access. "I was the royal prince." No one questioned his loyalty because of his family name. Case: "I was trusted at the highest levels of Hamas" — precisely because he was the son of a leader.

### Technique 2: The Prison Recruitment
In Israeli prison, the Shin Bet identified Mosab as a potential asset. They didn't just threaten him — they showed him the reality of what Hamas was doing and appealed to his conscience. Case: "Number 823" — from prisoner to agent, the transformation happened in the darkness of a cell.

### Technique 3: The Double Life
Mosab lived two lives simultaneously. By day, he was a Hamas loyalist. By night (or in secret meetings), he was an informant. "Trust no one" was the rule. Case: He attended Hamas strategy meetings and reported intelligence that foiled dozens of suicide bombings.

### Technique 4: Bible Study as a Weapon
In prison, reading the Bible gave Mosab a framework for peace that Islam (as practiced by Hamas) could not. "Jesus taught me to love my enemies." Case: Chapter 15, "Damascus Road" — his conversion mirrored Paul's, a complete transformation of worldview.

### Technique 5: Total Separation
When Mosab's identity was finally exposed, he had to cut all ties. His father disowned him. His family was in danger. He fled to the United States. Case: "I know you see me as a traitor; please understand it was not you I chose to betray."

### Technique 6: Telling Your Own Story
Rather than let others define him as a traitor or a hero, Mosab wrote his own account. "The events in this book are my best recollections, sorted out from the maelstrom of my life." Case: The book itself is an act of taking control of his narrative.

### Technique 7: The Moral Framework
Throughout the book, Mosab returns to a central question: "How many lives have I saved?" This is his anchor — the moral justification for everything he did. Case: "Every drop of innocent blood that has been saved gives me hope to carry on to the last day."
