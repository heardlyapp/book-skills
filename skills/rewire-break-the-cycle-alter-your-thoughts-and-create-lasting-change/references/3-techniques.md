# Techniques: The 7-Step Rewiring Protocol

> Source: Rewire, Phase 2

## The 7-Step Subconscious Rewiring Protocol

**Step 1 — Leave Your Phone Alone:** The first 30 minutes of the day set the neural tone. No phone for 30 minutes after waking. Use this time for intention-setting, visualization, or simply being.

**Step 2 — Visualization:** Michael Phelps' coach: "By the time Michael gets up on the blocks, he's swum that race hundreds of times in his mind." He visualized every outcome including his goggles filling with water — which happened in 2008. He swam blind for 175 meters, won gold, and broke the world record.

**Step 3 — Repetition:** A new habit takes 18-254 days (Lally, 2010). 66-day average for automaticity. Daily meditation, routines, and support groups build repetition.

**Step 4 — Make Space:** The space between trigger and response grows with practice. Nicole's gym receptionist: told she could only use coins for water. Her amygdala wanted to spiral. She paused, diverted the neural track, smiled: "Can we find a solution?" Her motto: Solutions not problems.

**Step 5 — Push Through Boundaries:** Motivation wanes. Discipline keeps going. Rely on routine, not willpower. Reduce cognitive load by cutting non-essentials.

**Step 6 — Create a Strategy:** Journaling, visualization, self-hypnosis, habit trackers, guided meditation, coach check-ins. Rehearse alternate responses.

**Step 7 — Step Through Fear:** Fear is not the enemy. Fear is a reason to act. Courage is not absence of fear — it's acknowledging fear and moving forward anyway.
