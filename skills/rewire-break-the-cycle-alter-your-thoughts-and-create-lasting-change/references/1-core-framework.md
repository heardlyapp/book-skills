# Core Framework: Neuroplasticity and the 3-Phase Protocol

> Source: Rewire, Introduction - Phase 1

## Neuroplasticity

The brain is malleable throughout life. Neurons that fire together wire together. Every thought, habit, and belief is a neural pathway that can be strengthened or weakened by attention and repetition. Adult neuroplasticity demands directed attention + repetition + sleep consolidation — you cannot learn passively.

> **Case: The Piano Experiment (Pascual-Leone et al., 1995):** Participants who mentally rehearsed a piano sequence showed the same cortical map changes as those who physically practiced. Thought alone can change the brain. But physical practice produced faster execution — showing that thought plus action is most powerful.

> **Case: New Habit Formation Time (Lally et al., 2010):** A new habit takes 18-254 days to form, with a 66-day average for automaticity. This is not a 3-week transformation. You are dismantling years of subconscious programming.

## The 3-Phase Protocol

**Phase 1: Ditch the Negative** — Identify and dismantle automatic negative patterns, stress responses, and cognitive distortions. Includes: stress triage, negativity bias protocol, affect labeling, creeping normality check.

**Phase 2: Shift Your Narrative** — Proactively rewire subconscious beliefs through a 7-step protocol: phone-free mornings, visualization, repetition, make space, push boundaries, create strategy, step through fear.

**Phase 3: Boost the Positive** — Support the rewired brain with biological foundations: growth mindset, exercise, sleep optimization, dopamine management.
