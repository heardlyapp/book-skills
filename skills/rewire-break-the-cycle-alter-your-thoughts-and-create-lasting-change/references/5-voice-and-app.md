# Voice and Application: Vignola's Approach

> Source: Rewire, throughout

## Vignola's Voice

Nicole Vignola writes as a neuroscientist and coach — accessible but evidence-based. She blends research citations (Lieberman, Crum, Dweck, Pascual-Leone, Lally) with practical protocols. Her central message: you are not broken. Your brain adapted to your environment. It can adapt again.

## Application: Breaking Habits

Use the 3-phase protocol as a 90-day program. Phase 1 (weeks 1-3): ditch the negative patterns. Phase 2 (weeks 4-10): build new neural pathways. Phase 3 (weeks 11-12): reinforce with biology.

## Application: Stress Management

The stress triage is the most immediately applicable tool. Most people don't know the difference between alert stress (good for performance), acute adaptive stress (builds resilience), and maladaptive chronic stress (needs intervention).

## Application: Building Self-Trust

Confidence is built through kept promises. Start with one small promise — waking up when your alarm goes off, not scrolling in bed, going for a walk. Each kept promise deposits into your self-trust account.

## Application: Sleep Optimization

Sleep is not optional for rewiring. Deep sleep releases growth hormone and clears brain waste. REM processes emotions and releases testosterone. Without ≥7 hours, plasticity cannot stabilize.
