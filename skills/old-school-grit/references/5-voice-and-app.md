## Key Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Wanting to Quit
Use the Never-Quit Protocol. Wait 24 hours. Ask: "Will I regret quitting more than continuing?"

### Scenario 2: Lack of Discipline
Start with one small daily non-negotiable. Do it every day. Build the habit, then add more.

### Scenario 3: Facing Adversity
Reframe it as a test. Ask: "What can I learn from this?" Take one small action forward.

### Scenario 4: Negative Mindset
Use the Attitude Shift Technique. Find one positive angle. Focus on what you can control.

### Scenario 5: Comparing Yourself to Others
Stop. Your only competition is who you were yesterday. Run your own race.
