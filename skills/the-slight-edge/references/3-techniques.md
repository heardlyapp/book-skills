# Techniques · Daily Practices

> Source: *The Slight Edge*

---

## The Daily 5 (or similar practice)

Pick 5 simple daily disciplines that move you toward your goal. Do them every day. No exceptions.

**Examples of daily disciplines:**
- Read 10 pages of a good book
- Walk 15 minutes
- Write down 3 things you're grateful for
- Make one connection call
- Review your goals for 2 minutes

## Morning Reflection / Evening Review

Start the day by reviewing your Slight Edge choices. End the day by checking if you did them.

## The 5% Principle

If you improve by just 5% in one area each year, in 5 years you'll be dramatically better. Small increments compound.
