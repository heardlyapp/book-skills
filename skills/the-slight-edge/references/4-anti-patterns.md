# Anti-Patterns · Why People Quit

> Source: *The Slight Edge*

---

## Impatience
Wanting results NOW. The Slight Edge requires time. No one sees the curve until they've been on it long enough.

## The Plateau of Latent Potential
You're doing the right things but see no results. Most people quit here. The plateau is where the compounding is happening silently.

## Confusing Knowledge with Action
Reading this book doesn't change your life. Doing what it says does.
