# Core Framework · The Slight Edge Philosophy

> Source: *The Slight Edge*

---

## What Is the Slight Edge?

The Slight Edge is a philosophy of success based on one truth: **simple, daily, positive activities repeated over time create massive success. Simple, daily, negative activities repeated over time create massive failure.**

> *"The Slight Edge is the philosophy that makes every other philosophy work."*

## The Slight Edge Curve

In the beginning, the Slight Edge choices (reading 10 pages, walking 15 minutes) show no visible difference from not doing them. This is where most people quit. Over time, the gap becomes a chasm.

**The curve has 3 phases:**
1. **The Invisible Phase** — No visible difference (most quit here)
2. **The Breakthrough Phase** — Results start to show
3. **The Exponential Phase** — Results compound dramatically

## The 7 Principles (Overview)
1. Show up every day
2. Be consistent (not intense)
3. Have a positive outlook
4. Be committed for the long haul
5. Take ownership (no excuses)
6. Keep learning and growing
7. Stay persistent through the plateau
