## The Gap Between Knowing and Doing

Everyone knows what to do: eat right, exercise, save money, read books. The gap is between knowledge and consistent action. The Slight Edge closes that gap.

**Why people fail:**
- They expect immediate results
- They overestimate what they can do in a month and underestimate what they can do in a year
- They quit during the invisible phase

## Time and Choice

Every moment presents a choice. Each choice pushes you slightly up or slightly down on the curve. Over days, the difference is invisible. Over years, it's everything.
