# Anti-Patterns

## 1. Practicing Without Purpose

**The mistake**: Going to the range and hitting ball after ball without a specific goal.

**Fix**: Every practice session should have a specific focus. "I'm working on 100-yard approach shots with feedback on dispersion."

## 2. Playing Hero Golf

**The mistake**: Trying difficult shots you can execute 1 in 10 times instead of the smart play.

**Fix**: Choose the shot you can execute 8 out of 10 times. The hero shot is for highlight reels, not scoring.

> **Case: The Hero Shot Addict** (Part II): A 10-handicap constantly tried to carry hazards rather than laying up. He could execute the hero shot maybe 20% of the time. When he laid up, his scores dropped immediately. The hero shot was costing him 3-4 strokes per round.
> **Key takeaway**: The smart play is almost always the boring play. Hero shots feel great when they work but cost more strokes than they save.

> **Case: The Frustrated Putter** (Part IV): A golfer who three-putted frequently tried to hole every long putt, leaving the ball past the hole and often missing the comebacker. When he focused on lag putting — getting every putt within a 2-foot circle — his three-putts dropped from 3 per round to 1.
>
> **Key takeaway**: Putting strategy is about leaving yourself a makeable second putt, not trying to hole every long putt. Lag putting saves more strokes than dead-at-the-pin putting.

## 3. Aiming at Every Pin

**The mistake**: Aiming at the flag on every approach shot.

**Fix**: Aim for the center of the green. The flag is a target for the best players in the world. For the rest of us, the center of the green is the right target.

## 4. Getting Emotional About Bad Shots

**The mistake**: Letting one bad shot ruin the next three holes.

**Fix**: The next shot is the only one that matters. Develop a reset routine after bad shots.

## 5. Comparing Yourself to Better Players

**The mistake**: Measuring your game against scratch golfers or tour pros.

**Fix**: The only comparison that matters is between your current game and your game from last month.
