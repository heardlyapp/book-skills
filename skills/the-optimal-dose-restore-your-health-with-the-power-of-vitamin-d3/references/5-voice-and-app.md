# Voice and Application: Somerville's Perspective

> Source: The Optimal Dose, Introduction - Chapter 8

## Somerville's Voice

Dr. Judson Somerville writes as a practicing physician who discovered vitamin D3's power first through his own health struggle, then through treating thousands of patients. He is passionate about optimal dosing and frustrated with standard medical guidelines that he believes harm patients.

## Application: Starting Your Protocol

Get a blood test for 25-hydroxyvitamin D. Calculate your starting dose (30-50 IU per lb of body weight). Take with a fatty meal and magnesium. Re-test after 3 months. Adjust until you reach 50-80 ng/mL.

## Application: Immune Support

Vitamin D3 is essential for immune function. Adequate levels support the body's defense against infections, including respiratory viruses. This is especially important during winter months.

## Application: Sleep Improvement

D3 taken in the morning helps regulate the circadian rhythm. Many patients report deeper, more restorative sleep within weeks of reaching optimal blood levels. Take D at breakfast, not bedtime.

## Application: Seasonal Health

Winter syndrome — low mood, low energy, frequent infections — is largely preventable with optimal D3 supplementation. Increase your dose in fall and winter when sun exposure decreases.
