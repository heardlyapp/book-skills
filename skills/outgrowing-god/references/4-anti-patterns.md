# Anti-Patterns — Outgrowing God

## The Central Error: "Without God, There Is No Morality"

This is the most common objection. Dawkins: "We don't need God to be good." Morality evolved. We have empathy because we're social animals. We make moral decisions through reason, not revelation.

### Mistake 1: "Evolution is just a theory"
"In science, a 'theory' is not a guess. It's a well-tested explanation supported by evidence." Case: The theory of gravity is "just a theory" too.

### Mistake 2: "There are no transitional fossils"
"There are hundreds of transitional fossils." Case: Tiktaalik (fish to tetrapod), Archaeopteryx (dinosaur to bird), whales with legs.

### Mistake 3: "The Bible is historically accurate"
"The story of Noah is physically impossible." Case: Where did the water go? How did kangaroos get back to Australia? The Bible is not a science or history textbook.

### Mistake 4: "Atheists have no purpose in life"
"Purpose comes from within, not from above." Case: Dawkins finds purpose in understanding the universe, in family, in teaching.

### Mistake 5: "Religion is necessary for social order"
"Secular countries are among the most peaceful and generous." Case: Scandinavian countries are among the least religious and most peaceful.

### Mistake 6: "Faith is a virtue"
"Faith is belief without evidence. That's not a virtue — it's a vice." Case: "If someone told you they had faith that the moon is made of cheese, you wouldn't admire their faith."
