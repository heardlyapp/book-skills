# 3 — Solutions: What Would Fix Washington

> Source: "How to Test Negative for Stupid" by John Kennedy

## Kennedy's Proposed Fixes

1. **Term limits for Congress.** Not a new idea, but Kennedy argues it's the only way to break the career politician cycle.
2. **Read the bills.** A simple rule: you can't vote on a bill you haven't read. This would kill most legislation.
3. **Transparency.** All earmarks should be public. All lobbyist meetings should be public. Sunshine is the best disinfectant.
4. **Balanced budget amendment.** The government should have to live within its means, just like families do.
5. **Simplify the tax code.** Flat tax or fair tax. The current code benefits only those who can afford accountants and lawyers.

> **Case: The Earmark Story** (Chapter 6): Kennedy describes a transportation bill with thousands of earmarks. "There was $50 million for a bridge to an island with 17 people on it. Seventeen. They could have bought each of them a yacht for less." The earmark was in the bill because a powerful committee member wanted it.
> **Key takeaway**: Waste is not a bug — it's a feature. The system is designed to distribute benefits to those with power. The only fix is transparency.
