# Principles of Breakthrough Negotiation

**Principle 1: Don't React — Go to the Balcony.** Your emotions are your worst enemy in negotiation. The balcony gives you perspective.

**Principle 2: Don't Argue — Step to Their Side.** Arguing makes them dig in. Listening opens them up.

**Principle 3: Don't Reject — Reframe.** Rejection escalates conflict. Reframing opens possibilities.

**Principle 4: Don't Push — Build a Golden Bridge.** Pushing creates resistance. A golden bridge invites agreement.

**Principle 5: Don't Escalate — Use Power to Educate.** Escalation destroys relationships. Education builds them.

**Case: The Middle East Negotiations. Ury has mediated in the Middle East using these principles.**
