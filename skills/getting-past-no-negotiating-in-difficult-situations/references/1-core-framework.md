# The Breakthrough Strategy — Core Framework

Ury's five-step strategy is designed to break through the five barriers to cooperation.

**Barrier 1: Your Reaction → Step 1: Go to the Balcony.** When the other side attacks, you react emotionally. The balcony is a mental space where you can observe without reacting. Practice: pause, breathe, recognize your triggers.

**Barrier 2: Their Emotion → Step 2: Step to Their Side.** When the other side is angry or defensive, reason does not work. Listen. Acknowledge. Show understanding. Defuse their emotion.

**Barrier 3: Their Position → Step 3: Reframe.** When the other side is stuck on a position, change the subject. Ask about interests, not positions. Redirect from past to future.

**Barrier 4: Their Dissatisfaction → Step 4: Build a Golden Bridge.** When the other side sees no benefit in agreeing, address their unmet interests. Help them save face.

**Barrier 5: Their Power → Step 5: Use Power to Educate.** When the other side thinks they can win without you, use your BATNA to educate them.
