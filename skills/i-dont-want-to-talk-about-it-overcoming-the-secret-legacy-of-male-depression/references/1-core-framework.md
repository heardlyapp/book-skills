# Male Depression Framework — Covert vs Overt

Real distinguishes between overt and covert depression.

**Case: The Angry Man. A man who seems perpetually irritable may be depressed. His anger is a mask for pain.**

**Case: The Workaholic. A man who works obsessively is often avoiding emotional pain. Busyness is a defense.**

**Case: The Addict. Addiction is closely linked to male depression. Substances mask the pain.**

**Case: The Withdrawn. A man who retreats into silence is in covert depression. He has given up on connection.**

**Case: The Grandiose. A man who acts superior is hiding shame. Grandiosity is a defense against depression.**

**Case: The Suicide Stat. Men are four times more likely to die by suicide. Covert depression kills.**
