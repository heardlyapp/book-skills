# 2 — Principles

> Source: *LeBron* by Jeff Benedict.

## 6 Principles

1. **Hire loyalty, train for skill.** The Four Horsemen had no experience. LeBron trained them. They never left.
2. **Your origin story is your power.** LeBron never hid Akron. He built a school there.
3. **When you fail, don't deflect.** The Decision was a disaster. LeBron took the hit to protect Carter.
4. **A billionaire who remembers where he came from is unstoppable.** LeBron still calls Akron home. Still builds there.
5. **Use your voice when it costs you.** LeBron spoke out on Trayvon Martin in 2012, facing Fox News attacks. He didn't stop.
6. **The mother-child bond is the most powerful relationship in life.** LeBron's love for Gloria is the emotional center of everything.
