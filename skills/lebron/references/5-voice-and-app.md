# 5 — Voice & Scenarios

> Source: *LeBron* by Jeff Benedict.

## Voice

Benedict writes with journalist objectivity — admiring of LeBron's achievements but not hagiographic. He includes the ugly moments (The Decision, the doomed Heat start, the fighting over social media) alongside the triumphs.

## 5 Scenarios

### 1. The Person Building Their First Team

Hire for loyalty, not credentials. The Four Horsemen had no experience. They had trust.

### 2. The Person Recovering from a Public Failure

The Decision cost LeBron 3 years of reputation. He rebuilt it by winning, growing, and staying.

### 3. The Person Who Came From Nothing

Akron was LeBron's wound and his armor. He went back. He built the school. Let your origin be your anchor.

### 4. The Person With a Platform

Speaking out on Trayvon Martin in 2012 was risky. LeBron did it anyway. Platform is not for comfort.

### 5. The Person Who Wants to Build Beyond Their Job

LeBron is a billionaire. The basketball was just the beginning. The empire is the point.
