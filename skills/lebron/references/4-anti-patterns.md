# 4 — Anti-Patterns

> Source: *LeBron* by Jeff Benedict.

## 4 Mistakes

### 1. The Made-for-TV Decision

Never invite 13M people to watch you reject the city that raised you. The medium amplified the message.

### 2. "I'm Taking My Talents to South Beach"

Nine words that became a villain origin story. The best decisions don't need a prime-time special.

### 3. Burning Jerseys

When 17-year-old fans burn your jersey, you miscalculated your audience. Always consider the emotional impact.

### 4. Comic Sans Letter

Cavs owner Gilbert's public rant made him look worse than LeBron. Don't fire back when you're angry.
