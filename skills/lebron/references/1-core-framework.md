# 1 — Core Framework

> Source: *LeBron* by Jeff Benedict.

## The 4 Frameworks

### 1. The Four Horsemen

LeBron's inner circle: Maverick Carter (business partner/cousin), Rich Paul (agent), Randy Mims (chief of staff). Hired as teenagers. Still together decades later. Revolutionary.

> **Case: The Decision** (Chapter 1): LeBron trusted Carter with the ESPN special. It was a disaster. He never blamed Carter publicly. "LeBron viewed Carter more like a brother than a business associate."

### 2. Glo and Bron

Gloria James had LeBron at 16. She moved him from couch to couch, project to project. She gave him to the Walkers so he could survive. He never forgot.

### 3. From Akron to Icon

"I didn't like looking for trouble. But I was on the edge of falling to an abyss." LeBron used poverty as fuel.

### 4. Beyond Basketball

Springer, Apple, Blaze, SpringHill. Home in L.A. School in Akron. "If he were an IPO, I'd buy in." — Warren Buffett
