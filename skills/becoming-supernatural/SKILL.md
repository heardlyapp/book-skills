## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **State Change**: Your brain waves (beta → alpha → theta → delta) determine your reality. To create change, shift from analytical beta to creative theta.
- **The Present Moment**: The only place where creation happens. In the past, you're a memory. In the future, you're a projection. In the now, you're pure potential.
- **Heart Coherence**: When your heart rhythm, brain waves, and nervous system synchronize, you tune into the quantum field. Measurable by HRV (heart rate variability).
- **Reconditioning**: Thinking a new thought is easy. Making the body feel that new thought as reality — that's reconditioning. The body must be convinced.
- **Space-Time vs. Time-Space**: In ordinary reality (space-time), you move through space and time. In meditation (time-space), you access the quantum field where all potentials exist simultaneously — and you choose one.
- **Quantum Field**: The invisible realm of infinite potentials. By changing your energy and intention, you collapse a new potential into your reality.

## Key Principles

1. **Change begins in the unseen before it appears in the seen.** Your internal state (thoughts, feelings, energy) creates your external reality. Work on the invisible first.
2. **Your body is the unconscious mind.** If your body knows the old self (through conditioned emotional responses), you will keep recreating the same life. Recondition the body to believe in the new reality.
3. **Surrender is not giving up — it's giving in to a greater intelligence.** When you stop trying to force outcomes and trust the quantum field, transformation accelerates.
4. **Elevated emotions are the key to tuning into new potentials.** Gratitude, joy, awe, love — these are not just feelings, they're frequencies that connect you to possibilities that fear and doubt cannot reach.
5. **The pineal gland is your biological connection to the quantum.** It translates energy from the field into your nervous system. Protect it (fluoride, calcification) and activate it through meditation.
6. **You must become the person who already has what you want, before it appears.** The body must feel the future as reality, not wait for external evidence.
7. **Case studies are proof that the impossible is possible.** Dispenza documents hundreds of healings. Use them as inspiration, not comparison. Your journey is unique.

## Anti-Pattern Summary

The core mistake this book corrects: **the belief that you are a victim of your biology, genetics, and past experiences — when in fact you have the power to change your brain, body, and life by changing your internal state, because consciousness creates reality, not the other way around.**
