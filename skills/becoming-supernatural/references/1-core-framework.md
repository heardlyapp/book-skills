# 1 — The Becoming Supernatural Framework

> Source: "Becoming Supernatural: How Common People Are Doing the Uncommon" by Dr. Joe Dispenza

## The Core Framework: State → Present Moment → Quantum → New Reality

### Step 1: Change Your State (Brain Waves)

The framework begins with the understanding that your ordinary waking state (beta brain waves) keeps you locked in your known reality. To access new potentials, you must shift into alpha (relaxed awareness) and theta (deep meditative state).

> **Case: Brain Scans of Meditators** (Introduction): Dispenza's team used EEG to measure the brain activity of thousands of participants during advanced retreats. They documented real-time shifts from beta to alpha to theta — and in some cases, delta — during the Blessing of the Energy Centers meditation. Participants reported transcendent experiences that were measurable in their brain waves.
> **Key takeaway**: The shift in consciousness is not subjective — it's measurable. Your brain literally changes its operating frequency when you enter the supernatural state.

### Step 2: Enter the Present Moment

In the present moment, the brain is not running old programs. The past is a memory (beta). The future is a projection (beta). Only the now is empty of the known — and therefore open to the new.

> **Case: The Rock Geshe** (Foreword): Gregg Braden tells the story of a Tibetan geshe who meditated in a cave. When asked how he could spend years in isolation, he said: "I am part of the rock, not separate from it. The rock cannot contain me." He had transcended the limited sense of self that feels trapped by circumstances.
> **Key takeaway**: When you are fully present, you dissolve the boundary between self and environment. That dissolution is freedom.

### Step 3: Heart Coherence

Once the brain quiets, the heart takes over. HeartMath Institute research shows that when the heart rhythm becomes coherent (smooth, ordered wave pattern), it broadcasts a signal to the brain and the rest of the body that says "it's safe to heal."

> **Case: HeartMath Partnership** (Introduction): Dispenza partnered with HeartMath Institute to measure the heart rate variability of thousands of participants. They found that during the meditations, participants' heart rhythms shifted from chaotic (stress) to coherent (harmonized). This coherence is the biological gateway to the quantum field.
> **Key takeaway**: Heart coherence is not a metaphor. It's a measurable state where the heart, brain, and nervous system work in harmony. In this state, the body can heal.

### Step 4: Tune into the Quantum Field

Quantum physics tells us that at the subatomic level, all potential realities exist simultaneously — until observation collapses the wave function into a specific reality. Dispenza's framework uses meditation as the means to "observe" a new potential and collapse it into existence.

> **Case: Tuning In to New Potentials** (Chapter 3): Dispenza explains how quantum experiments show that particles exist in a state of all possibilities until observed. In the same way, he argues, by changing our internal state (energy, intention, feeling), we "observe" a new reality into being. The process is not magic — it's quantum mechanics applied to consciousness.
> **Key takeaway**: Reality is not fixed. At the level where matter meets energy, you have a vote. Your consciousness is the observer that collapses possibility into actuality.

### Step 5: Recondition the Body to the New Mind

The body has been conditioned by years of the old self. It knows how to feel stressed, sick, limited. To become supernatural, you must teach the body a new emotional state — one that matches the new reality you want to create.

> **Case: Spontaneous Remissions** (Chapters 6, 10, 14): Dispenza documents dozens of case studies of people who healed from conditions considered "incurable" — breast cancer, Parkinson's, chronic pain, autoimmune disorders. The common thread: they all learned to change their internal state from fear/hopelessness to gratitude/wholeness, and the body followed.
> **Key takeaway**: When the body knows the new mind — when it feels healthy, whole, and free at the cellular level — biology rearranges itself to match that state. Healing is the byproduct of being whole.
