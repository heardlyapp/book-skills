# 1 — The Carnivore Framework

> Source: "Eat What You Kill" by Sam Taggart

## The Core Framework: Mindset → Strategy → Execution

### The Carnivore Spectrum

Taggart divides salespeople into three categories: **Herbivores** (wait for leads), **Carnivores** (hunt their own deals), and **Omnivores** (can do both but rely on carnivore skills for their best results). The Carnivore is always self-reliant, never dependent on a lead pipeline.

> **Case: The Golf Ball Business** (Introduction): At age 8, Taggart fished golf balls out of ponds, cleaned them, and sold them to golfers on weekends. He soon added chips and lemonade. "Some Saturdays I went home with a hundred bucks, which was serious money!" When the golf course banned him, he didn't stop — he found a new way. This is the carnivore mindset in action: find an opportunity, pursue it relentlessly, adapt when blocked.
> **Key takeaway**: Carnivores are not born — they're made. Taggart was hunting deals at age 8 because he discovered the thrill of creating his own income.

> **Case: The Curb Painting Empire** (Introduction): At 13, Taggart painted house numbers on curbs for $20 per house. He recruited 11 other kids to do the same, taking $8 per sale from each rep. He wrote scripts, managed schedules, and bought supplies. At 14, he was a sales manager running a team. "I wasn't just a salesman, I was a sales manager."
> **Key takeaway**: The carnivore mentality extends beyond selling — it's about building systems. Taggart didn't just sell; he systematized his success by recruiting and training others.

### The Mindset Spectrum: Victim → Survivor → Conqueror

Taggart identifies three mindsets: **Victim** (blames circumstances, waits for rescue), **Survivor** (gets by, doesn't thrive), **Conqueror** (takes full responsibility, hunts relentlessly).

### The Mental Governor

Every salesperson has a built-in limiter — a "mental governor" that tells you you've done enough, that the rejection hurts too much, that you should quit for the day. The governor is your brain's misguided attempt to protect you from discomfort. The only way past it is action — keep moving, and the governor resets.

### Prey Drive

Intrinsic hunger that makes you pursue deals. Taggart argues you can't teach prey drive — you can only activate it, protect it, and keep it fed by celebrating wins.
