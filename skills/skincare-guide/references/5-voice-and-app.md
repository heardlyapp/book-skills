# 5 — Hirons' Voice + Application

> Source: "Skincare" by Caroline Hirons

## Hirons' Voice

Caroline Hirons is a British skincare expert with 20+ years in the industry. Her voice: no-BS, straight-talking, often hilarious. She has no patience for marketing fluff and calls out brands by name. She's known for her "Cheat Sheets" and no-nonsense advice.

> **Case: The Introduction** (Introduction): Hirons explains why she wrote this book: "I'm tired of people being ripped off by brands that sell hope in a jar. I'm tired of people spending money they don't have on products that don't work. This book is everything I've learned in 20 years."
> **Key takeaway**: Hirons is on the consumer's side. She doesn't represent any brand. Her loyalty is to evidence and results.

## Key Quote

"Sunscreen is not negotiable. If you don't wear SPF every day, nothing else matters."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Complete beginner** → Start with the Core Four. Cleanse, treat, moisturize, protect. Master this before adding anything.
2. **Acne-prone adult** → Gentle routine. BHA, niacinamide. No harsh scrubs. Hydrate.
3. **Aging concerns** → Retinol (start low). Vitamin C (AM). Sunscreen (every day). Consistency.
4. **Sensitive skin** → Fragrance-free. Minimal ingredients. Patch test everything. Repair the barrier first.
5. **Budget skincare** → Drugstore cleansers, simple moisturizers, sunscreen. Effective products exist at every price point.
