# 5 — Folklore and Reference

> Source: "The 2026 Old Farmer's Almanac"

## The Almanac's Voice

The Old Farmer's Almanac has been published continuously since 1792. Its voice: warm, witty, practical, and slightly old-fashioned. It speaks to a reader who values tradition, self-reliance, and connection to the natural world.

## Key Quote

"Knowledge is like a garden — if it is not cultivated, it cannot be harvested."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Planning a garden** → Know your frost dates. Test your soil. Start small.
2. **Preparing for a trip** → Check the Almanac's long-range forecast for your destination.
3. **Stargazing** → Check moon phases and meteor shower dates. Plan around the full moon.
4. **Preserving the harvest** → Follow tested canning procedures. Don't guess.
5. **Living more seasonally** → Eat what grows when it grows. Plan your year around the seasons, not the calendar.
