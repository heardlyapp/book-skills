# 2 — Gardening and Planting

> Source: "The 2026 Old Farmer's Almanac"

## Planting by the Frost Dates

The single most important piece of gardening information is your average last spring frost date. Everything else depends on this.

## Planting by the Moon

The Almanac provides a detailed planting calendar based on moon phases. Above-ground crops are planted during the waxing moon. Below-ground crops during the waning moon.

> **Case: The Frost Date Maps** (Gardening Section): The Almanac provides detailed maps showing average last and first frost dates for every region. "Knowing your frost dates is more important than knowing your zip code."
> **Key takeaway**: Frost dates are the foundation of garden planning. Everything else — seed starting, transplanting, harvest — flows from this.

> **Case: The Best Days for Planting** (Moon Tables): The Almanac's "Best Days" tables tell you the best days for planting specific crops based on the moon's position. "Plant root crops in the dark of the moon. Plant above-ground crops in the light of the moon."
> **Key takeaway**: Whether or not moon planting works, it's a useful framework for organizing your garden work. And many experienced gardeners swear by it.
