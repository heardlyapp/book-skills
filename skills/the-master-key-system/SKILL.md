## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **The 24 Lessons:** One per week. Each builds on the previous. Cover everything from the nature of thought to health, wealth, and spiritual growth.
- **The Universal Mind:** The infinite intelligence that pervades everything. Individual mind is a channel for this universal force.
- **The Law of Attraction:** Like attracts like. By holding a thought, you attract corresponding conditions.
- **The Creative Process:** (1) Idealize (form a mental image), (2) Visualize (hold it clearly), (3) Realize (bring it into being through action).
- **The Daily Practice:** Spend 15-30 minutes daily in silence. Visualize your ideal. Affirm your connection to the Universal.
- **The Master Key:** The knowledge that the power to create your reality lies within your own mind.
- **The Subconscious Mind:** The creative power within. Impressions made on the subconscious are expressed in the outer world. The daily practice trains the subconscious to align with your ideals.
- **Silence and Meditation:** The essential practice. In silence, we connect with the Universal Mind. The still mind is the channel for inspiration.
- **The Law of Compensation:** What you give out comes back. Service to others is the foundation of lasting success.
- **The Interconnectedness of All Things:** Separation is an illusion. We are all one in the Universal Mind.

## Key Principles (7 Rules)

1. **Everything begins with thought.** The outer world is a reflection of the inner world.
2. **Align with the Universal Mind.** Individual effort without universal alignment is limited.
3. **Hold your ideal clearly.** Vague thoughts produce vague results.
4. **Practice daily silence.** The still mind is the channel for inspiration.
5. **Think abundance.** Scarcity thinking attracts scarcity. Abundance thinking attracts abundance.
6. **Act on your inspiration.** Thought without action is fantasy. The universe works through you.
7. **Know yourself as part of the whole.** You are not separate — you are a fragment of the infinite.
