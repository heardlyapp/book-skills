# The Universal Mind and Individual Thought

> Source: The Master Key System, Parts 1-8

## The Fundamental Principles

### Mind as the Creative Force

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Universal Mind | The infinite intelligence that pervades all of existence |
| Individual mind | A fragment of the Universal, with the same creative power |
| Thought | The seed of all creation |
| Subconscious mind | The creative power within that manifests thought into form |

> **Case: The Universal Mind (Part 4)**
> Haanel writes: "The Universal Mind is the life principle of every atom, every molecule, every cell. It is the principle of life. It is the intelligence that gives life to all things." The individual mind is not separate — it is a channel through which the Universal Mind expresses. This is why thought has power: your thought is not just your thought. It is the Universal Mind thinking through you.
> **Key takeaway:** The Universal Mind concept solves the problem of individual power. You are not creating reality from your limited self — you are aligning with infinite intelligence. The key is not to strain and effort — it is to align and allow.

> **Case: The Subconscious as Creative Power (Part 8)**
> The subconscious mind is the creative power within. It does not reason — it acts. It takes whatever dominant thought you hold and brings it into form. This is why visualization works: when you repeatedly present a mental image to the subconscious, it accepts it as reality and works to produce it. The conscious mind chooses the ideal. The subconscious brings it into being.
> **Key takeaway:** The conscious-subconscious partnership is the mechanism of creation. The conscious mind directs. The subconscious executes. Together, they are the creative power of the individual.
