# Anti-Patterns · Why Habits Fail

> Source: *Tiny Habits*, full book

---

## 1. Self-Blame
"I must be lazy / undisciplined / weak." No. If a behavior doesn't happen, your design is wrong. Redesign the 3 elements.

## 2. Waiting for Motivation
"I'll start when I feel like it." Motivation is the least reliable element. Design for ability instead.

## 3. Too Big, Too Soon
"Run 5km daily" / "Read for 30 minutes" / "Meditate for 20 minutes." These aren't habits — they're aspirations. Shrink them first.

## 4. No Celebration
Skipping celebration is like skipping the glue that bonds the habit. Without positive emotion, the habit won't wire in.

## 5. Wrong Anchor
Picking a prompt that doesn't exist yet. "After I finish my morning yoga" — but you don't do morning yoga.

## 6. Aspirations vs Behaviors
"I want to be healthy" is an aspiration, not a behavior. Convert it: "I will eat one vegetable at dinner."
