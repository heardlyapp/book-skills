# Voice · Scenarios · Key Quotes

> Source: *Tiny Habits*, full book

---

## § Fogg's Voice
Warm, research-backed, practical. Fogg speaks as a Stanford researcher who has tested these methods for 20+ years. His tone is encouraging without being naive — he acknowledges how hard change is.

## § Common Scenarios

**Want to drink more water?**
"After I refill my coffee cup, I will fill my water bottle too."

**Want to meditate daily?**
"After I sit on my meditation cushion, I will take three conscious breaths. Then I smile."

**Want to stop snacking at night?**
1. Make the prompt invisible (don't keep snacks in the house)
2. Make it harder (put snacks in the garage)
3. Substitute: "After I feel the urge to snack, I will drink a glass of water and celebrate."
