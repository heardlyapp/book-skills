# Anti-Patterns — Misunderstanding Stoicism

**Anti-Pattern 1: Stoicism Is Passivity. Stoics are not passive. They act on what they can control.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: Stoicism Is Emotionlessness. Stoics feel emotions. They do not let emotions control them.**

**Case: The Emotion Suppression. Suppressing emotions is not Stoic. Understanding them is.**

**Case: The Fatalism. Stoics do not just accept fate. They embrace it as necessary.**

**Case: The Detachment. Stoics do not detach from life. They engage fully with virtue.**

**Case: The Modern Self-Help. Stoicism is not just ancient self-help. It is a comprehensive philosophy.**
