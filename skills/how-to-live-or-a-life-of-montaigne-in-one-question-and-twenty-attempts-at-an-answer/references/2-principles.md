# Principles from Montaigne

**Principle 1: Doubt Everything. Certainty is dangerous. Doubt is honest. Montaigne's skepticism was a survival strategy.**

**Principle 2: Know Yourself. The essays are a portrait of a human being. Montaigne believed knowing yourself is the foundation of wisdom.**

**Principle 3: Embrace Your Body. Montaigne wrote about his body frankly: his kidneys, his digestion, his sexuality.**
