# 5 — Ruiz's Voice + Application

> Source: "The Mastery of Love" by Don Miguel Ruiz

## Ruiz's Voice

Don Miguel Ruiz is a Mexican author and spiritual teacher. His voice: simple, profound, compassionate. He writes in short chapters with clear teachings and metaphors. His books have sold millions worldwide.

## Key Quote

"Love is not something you get. It's what you are."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Single and looking for love** → First, love yourself. Then you'll attract relationships that match that standard.
2. **In a painful relationship** → Heal your wounds first. The relationship will either heal with you or end — either is progress.
3. **After a breakup** → Grieve. Heal. Forgive. The relationship served its purpose. Learn from it and move on.
4. **Jealous or controlling** → Look inward. Your fear is not about your partner — it's about your own self-worth.
5. **Desperate for love** → The love you seek is already within you. Stop looking outside. Start looking inward.
