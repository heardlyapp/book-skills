# Voice and Application: Lafley and Martin's Approach

> Source: Playing to Win, throughout

## Their Voice

Lafley and Martin write as practitioner and professor. Lafley brings real CEO experience from leading P&G's transformation; Martin brings theoretical rigor from Rotman School of Management. Together they make strategy accessible, concrete, and practical. The book is full of specific P&G examples.

> **Case: The Practitioner-Professor Partnership** (Introduction): They met as a category manager and consultant, then worked together for 20+ years through P&G's transformation. This frames the book as a genuine collaboration between practice and theory.

> **Case: The Olay Story** (Chapter 1): The detailed Olay turnaround case — from declining brand to billion-dollar business — shows the framework in action. Every strategic choice is illustrated with real data and real results.

> **Key takeaway**: The best strategy books come from people who have done it. Lafley's P&G experience gives the framework real credibility.
