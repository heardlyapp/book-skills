## The Legacy

Chariots of the Gods has sold millions of copies and been translated into dozens of languages. It inspired the History Channel's "Ancient Aliens" series.

**Case: The Mainstream Rejection.** Mainstream archaeologists have consistently rejected von Däniken's claims. The book is not considered valid science by the academic establishment.

**Case: The Popular Appeal.** Despite the rejection, the book continues to sell. It speaks to a human desire for mystery and wonder that official history does not satisfy.
