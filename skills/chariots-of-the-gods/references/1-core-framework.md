# The Ancient Astronaut Theory — Core Framework

Von Däniken's central claim: ancient humans were visited by extraterrestrials who were mistaken for gods. The evidence is everywhere — in stone, in text, in legend.

**Case: The Pyramids.** The Great Pyramid contains blocks up to 80 tons, cut with precision to 1/100th of an inch. Alignment to true north is accurate to 0.067 degrees. Internal passages align with specific stars. Von Däniken: this required technology beyond ancient capability.

**Case: The Nazca Lines.** Giant figures in the Peruvian desert visible only from the air. A monkey, hummingbird, spider, condor. Lines stretch perfectly straight for miles. Von Däniken: landing markers for spacecraft.

**Case: Easter Island.** The moai statues weigh up to 80 tons. How were they moved across the island? Conventional explanations (logs, sledges) are inadequate. Island legends speak of statues that "walked" or "flew."

**Case: The Piri Reis Map.** An Ottoman admiral's 1513 map showing the coastline of Antarctica — not discovered until 1818. Von Däniken: this could only have been made with aerial photography or satellite imagery.

**Case: The Baalbek Terrace.** In Lebanon, the Baalbek temple complex sits on massive stone blocks weighing up to 1,000 tons. How were these blocks moved and placed? Conventional archaeology has no satisfactory answer. Von Däniken: this required technology beyond ancient capabilities.
