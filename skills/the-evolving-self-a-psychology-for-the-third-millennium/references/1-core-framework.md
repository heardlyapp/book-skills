# Core Framework: The Evolution of Consciousness

> Source: The Evolving Self, Introduction and Chapters 1-2

## Beyond Biological Evolution

Csikszentmihalyi argues that biological evolution has been superseded by cultural and psychological evolution. We now evolve through ideas, values, and conscious choices about how we live.

> **Case: The Meme Framework** (Chapter 1): Just as genes carry biological information, "memes" carry cultural information — ideas, values, practices, beliefs. We can choose which memes to adopt and which to reject. This conscious selection is the next stage of human evolution.

> **Key takeaway**: We are no longer passive subjects of evolution. Through memes and conscious choice, we can actively guide our own psychological and cultural development.

## Psychic Entropy

The default state of consciousness is disorder — random thoughts, conflicting goals, scattered attention.

> **Case: The Attention Crisis** (Chapter 2): Csikszentmihalyi shows most people struggle to direct their attention. Psychic entropy takes forms: anxiety, boredom, apathy, distraction. The ability to invest attention in meaningful goals determines life quality.

> **Case: The Flow Exception** (Chapter 2): Flow is the rare state where consciousness is fully ordered. Psychic energy is completely focused on a clear goal. This ordered state produces growth, satisfaction, and complexity.

> **Key takeaway**: Psychic entropy is the natural default. Flow must be actively cultivated through increasing challenges and skills. Attention is the most valuable resource.
