# Core Framework: Menopause and Cellular Health

> Source: The Menopause Reset, Foreword and Chapters 1-3

## Menopause Is a Reset, Not a Disease

Dr. Pelz argues menopause is a natural transition where the body resets itself. Symptoms are signals, not a disease to be medicated away.

> **Case: Dr. Pelz's Own Journey** (Preface): Dr. Pelz experienced severe perimenopause — brain fog, weight gain, fatigue, anxiety, insomnia. She tried many treatments for thyroid and adrenals but found nothing worked until she addressed the root cause: cellular dysfunction.

> **Key takeaway**: Menopause symptoms are signals from your body. They're not something to suppress — they're something to understand and address at the cellular level.

## The Cellular Foundation

Hormone problems start at the cellular level. Fix the cell, fix the hormone.

> **Case: The Pompa Protocol** (Chapter 1): Dr. Pelz introduces the Pompa Protocol — a multi-therapeutic approach developed by Dr. Pompa that focuses on cellular health through fasting, ketosis, and detox. This addresses root causes rather than downstream symptoms.

> **Case: The Thyroid Connection** (Chapter 3): Many women are diagnosed with thyroid issues during menopause. Dr. Pelz argues that thyroid dysfunction is often a symptom of cellular dysfunction — fix the cell, and the thyroid often heals.

> **Key takeaway**: Downstream approaches (hormone replacement, symptom medication) have their place, but upstream cellular health is the true foundation. Fix the cell first.
