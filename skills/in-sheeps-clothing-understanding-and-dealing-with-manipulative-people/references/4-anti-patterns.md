# Anti-Patterns — What Makes You Vulnerable

**Anti-Pattern 1: Explaining Yourself. You do not need to justify your boundaries. No is a complete sentence.**

**Anti-Pattern 2: Expecting Fairness. Manipulators do not play fair. Expecting them to is a mistake.**

**Case: The Apology Trap. You apologize for setting boundaries. This gives the manipulator an opening.**

**Case: The Fixer. You try to help the manipulator change. They do not want to change.**

**Case: The People-Pleaser. Your need to be liked makes you an easy target.**

**Case: The Overthinker. You try to understand their motives. Understanding does not help. Action does.**
