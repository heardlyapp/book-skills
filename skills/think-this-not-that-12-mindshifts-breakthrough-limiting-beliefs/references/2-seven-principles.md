# Seven Principles in Depth

## Principle 1: Beliefs Are Self-Fulfilling Prophecies

**Case — Axe's teacher:** Mrs. Nobel laughed at his dream of becoming a doctor. Combined with his 2.3 GPA and ADHD diagnosis, this nearly destroyed his potential. The memory transplant with Mrs. Williams reversed it. **Case — Man who died of belief:** Diagnosed with terminal liver cancer, died within predicted timeframe. Autopsy showed only a small, non-life-threatening tumor.

## Principle 2: Becoming Over Accomplishing

**Case — Grandfather Howard:** No wealth, no fame. At his funeral, person after person called him "my best friend." Axe's formula: Success = maximizing your unique skill for the good of others. **Case — Lance Armstrong:** Seven Tour titles, but character collapse destroyed everything.

## Principle 3: Self-Awareness

**Case — Tasha Eurich's research:** 90% believe they are self-aware; 10-15% actually are. **Case — Batman Effect:** 23% improvement when children adopted a superhero identity. SWOT analysis adapted for personal growth.

## Principle 4: Purpose

**Case — Axe's mother's cancer:** Stage 4 at age 41. Her tumors shrank 52% in four months through diet + mindset. She water-skis at 68. **Case — STARS goal method:** Specific, Trackable, Accountable, Realistic, Significant.

## Principle 5: Rewrite Your Story

**Case — Harriet Tubman:** Born enslaved, beaten, seizures. Became Underground Railroad conductor. "She had every right to remain a victim." **Case — Axe's failed business:** Lost all savings. Relaunched to build Ancient Nutrition.

## Principle 6: Virtues and Principles

**Case — Virtue card method:** Axe wrote "encouragement" on an index card, practiced daily until it became natural. **Case — Dave Ramsey's financial principles:** Get out of debt, give 10%, invest 10%. Simple principles rebuilt Axe's wealth.

## Principle 7: Fear as Growth Signal

**Case — Axe's spinal infection:** Couldn't walk for a year, doctors said he might die. He chose hope, grit, gratitude. **Case — Sara Blakely:** Father celebrated her failures. She built Spanx through persistence.
