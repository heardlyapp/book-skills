# Core Framework: The 12 Mindshifts

## Overview

Josh Axe's framework is built around 12 specific mindshifts — conscious shifts from limiting beliefs to empowering ones. Each chapter follows the same structure: identify the false belief, understand where it came from, challenge it with evidence, and replace it with a truthful belief.

**M1 — Unlimited Beliefs:** Memory transplant technique. ABCDE model (Albert Ellis). Nocebo effect: the man who died from a non-life-threatening tumor because he believed he was dying. Jamie Kern Lima: told no one would buy her makeup; L'Oréal bought IT Cosmetics for $1.2B.

**M2 — Becoming Over Accomplishing:** Grandfather Howard's funeral: no wealth or fame but called "best friend" by dozens. Lance Armstrong: accomplishments without character collapse. Steve Jobs' regrets: "I wanted my kids to know me."

**M3 — Self-Awareness:** Tasha Eurich's research: 90% think they're self-aware, only 10-15% are. Batman Effect study: 23% improvement with superhero identity. SWOT for personal growth. Four barriers: pride, busyness, character sabotage, entertainment.

**M4 — Purpose:** Axe's mother's stage 4 breast cancer at 41 became his purpose. Her tumors shrank 52% in four months. STARS goal method.

**M5 — Rewrite Your Story:** Harriet Tubman: from enslaved to Underground Railroad conductor. Axe's failed business: lost all savings, relaunched to build Ancient Nutrition.

**M6 — Community of Greatness:** 6-8 people sharing purpose. Shawn Johnson and Andrew East story. Tolkien and C.S. Lewis's Inklings.

**M7 — Unshakable Identity:** Three foundations: modern (sand), traditional (soil), divine (stone). Carl Jung: "The world will ask you who you are."

**M8 — Virtues Over Vices:** Seven virtues vs. seven vices. Virtue card method: wrote "encouragement" on an index card, practiced daily.

**M9 — Principles Over Opinions:** 10 life principles. Warren Buffett ignores hype. Michael Burry predicted 2008 crash.

**M10 — Flip Fear:** Carol Dweck's fifth graders: praise for effort beats praise for intelligence. Axe's business failure led to Ancient Nutrition.

**M11 — Visualize:** Sara Blakely visualized Oprah before appearing. Free-throw study: mental rehearsal = 23% improvement vs 24% physical practice.

**M12 — Positive Perseverance:** Axe's spinal infection: couldn't walk for a year. Hope (Snyder) + Grit (Duckworth) + Gratitude (UC San Diego). Deion Sanders: from suicide attempt to coaching.
