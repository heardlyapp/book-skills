## Philosophy

The world has been programmed into you. Every belief, every value, every "should" — you absorbed it from the culturescape. Most of it is wrong.

The extraordinary mind does not accept the default programming. It questions everything, chooses consciously what to believe, and rewrites its own model of reality.

You are not just the product of your genes and environment. You are the programmer.

## Core Framework Quick Reference

1. **The Culturescape**: The invisible system of cultural norms, values, and beliefs that shapes every person from birth. Most people never question it.
2. **The Brules (Bullshit Rules)**: Society's unquestioned "shoulds" — go to college, get a job, climb the ladder, retire at 65. Most Brules no longer serve us.
3. **Consciousness Engineering**: The practice of consciously examining your beliefs and choosing which to keep, upgrade, or discard. You are not stuck with your current beliefs.
4. **Models of Reality**: The mental frameworks through which you interpret the world. Change the model, change the experience.
5. **Systems for Living**: Daily practices — meditation, exercise, learning, gratitude — that shape your experience. Upgrade the systems, upgrade your life.
6. **Bending Reality**: The state of flow, intuition, and synchronicity that occurs when you are aligned with your purpose and operating from a higher consciousness.
7. **Blissipline**: The discipline of maintaining happiness — not as a reaction to circumstances but as a daily practice.

## Key Principles

1. There is no objective reality — there is only the reality you have been programmed to see.
2. Most of what you believe is not yours — it was given to you. Question it.
3. Consciousness engineering is a skill. You can learn to choose your beliefs.
4. Gratitude and happiness are not outcomes — they are practices. Blissipline is the daily commitment to joy.
5. The most powerful state is flow — when you are fully present and aligned with your purpose.
6. Goals should come from vision, not fear. The extraordinary mind creates goals that excite it, not goals that pacify it.
7. You are capable of far more than you have been told. The limits are in your programming, not your potential.

## The Six-Phase Meditation

Vishen's signature practice (available via the Mindvalley app):
1. **Connect** — Attune to yourself
2. **Gratitude** — Feel appreciation for three things
3. **Forgiveness** — Release resentment
4. **Destiny** — Connect to your higher purpose
5. **Vision** — Visualize your desired future
6. **Bless** — Send love and blessing to the world
