## From Discovery to Delivery

**The process:**
1. Identify the problem (opportunity assessment)
2. Run discovery sprints to validate solution
3. Create a minimal viable product (not a prototype)
4. Build, measure, learn

## Prototyping Techniques
- **Paper prototypes**: Fastest way to test usability
- **Clickable prototypes**: Test flow and interaction
- **Live-data prototypes**: Test with real data
- **Concierge test**: Manually deliver the service to validate demand

## The Product Council
A monthly meeting where teams present what they've learned in discovery and get cross-functional input.
