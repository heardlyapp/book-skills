# Anti-Patterns · What Kills Product

> Source: *Inspired*, full book

---

## The Feature Factory
"The most common and destructive form of product development." Teams that just ship features without understanding if they solve real problems.

## HiPPO
The Highest Paid Person's Opinion driving product decisions. The antidote: data and discovery.

## Sales-Driven Roadmap
Building what the biggest customer asks for. This creates a product that solves one customer's problems but doesn't build a sustainable business.

## Waterfall Product Development
Design everything upfront, then build it. In software, this is almost always wrong.
