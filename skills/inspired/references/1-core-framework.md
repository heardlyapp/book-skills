# Core Framework · Product Vision · Product Discovery

> Source: *Inspired*, Parts 2-4

---

## Product Vision & Strategy

**Product Vision**: The long-term north star. Where the product will be in 2-5 years. It should be inspiring and stable.

**Product Strategy**: The path to the vision. Which customers to serve, which problems to solve, which products to build — and in what sequence.

**Product Principles**: Decision-making guardrails. A set of principles that guide day-to-day product decisions.

## Product Discovery

The process of validating product ideas before building them. Four critical questions:
1. **Value**: Will people buy or use this?
2. **Usability**: Can users figure out how to use it?
3. **Feasibility**: Can engineering build it with available time and tech?
4. **Viability**: Does it work for the business (legal, sales, finance)?

## Key Roles (Ch 1-9)
- Product Manager: Responsible for value and viability
- Product Designer: Responsible for usability
- Tech Lead: Responsible for feasibility
- Product Marketing: Responsible for go-to-market
