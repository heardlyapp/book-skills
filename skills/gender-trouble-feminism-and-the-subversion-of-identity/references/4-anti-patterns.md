# Common Mistakes — Anti-Patterns

**Anti-Pattern 1: Confusing Performativity with Performance.** Performativity is not a voluntary choice. You cannot simply decide to perform gender differently and change it. Norms constrain what performances are possible.

**Anti-Pattern 2: Biological Determinism.** Assuming sex is natural and only gender is constructed. Butler: sex is as constructed as gender.

**Case: The Choice Myth.** "I can choose my gender" misunderstands performativity. Gender norms are not chosen — they are imposed and internalized.

**Case: The Nature Fallacy.** The belief that sex is natural and unchanging ignores the history of how sex has been understood.
