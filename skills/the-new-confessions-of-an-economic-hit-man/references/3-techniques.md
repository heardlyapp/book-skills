# 3 — Techniques

> Source: *The New Confessions of an Economic Hit Man* by John Perkins.

## 4 Tools

### 1. The Loan Audit

When presented with a loan: ask who benefits. Who gets the contracts? What are the real interest rates? Can the borrower actually repay?

### 2. The EHM Forecast Check

Skeptical of economic forecasts? Ask: who paid for them? Are they conservative or aggressive? Do they assume unlimited growth?

### 3. The Book as Insurance

Perkins survived because the book made the truth public. The more people who know, the harder it is to silence.

> **Case: Perkins' Daughter** (Preface): "Don't worry, Dad. If they get you, I'll take over where you left off." His daughter's courage gave him the strength to publish.

### 4. The Transition to a Life Economy

Start locally. Cooperatives. Fair trade. Sustainability. Perkins' Dream Change Coalition.
