# 5 — Voice & App

> Source: *The New Confessions of an Economic Hit Man* by John Perkins.

## Voice

Perkins writes as a confessed insider — guilty, haunted, but hopeful. The voice is confessional, conspiratorial, and redemptive.

> **Case: The Leper's Warning** (Chapter 6): A leper thrust his bloody stump through Perkins' limousine window. "Reform. Repent." It became Perkins' turning point.

> **Case: Torrijos and Roldós** (Chapters 10, 26): Two presidents who refused EHM demands. Both died in plane crashes. "Their deaths were not accidental."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Evaluating a loan offer** — Use the loan audit
2. **Working for a company that feels wrong** — Perkins' example shows you can change
3. **Facing threats for speaking out** — Transparency is protection
4. **Building a sustainable business** — Cooperatives, fair trade, localism
5. **Understanding world events** — Look for the EHM dynamic behind the news
