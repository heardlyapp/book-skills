# 1 — Core Framework

> Source: *The New Confessions of an Economic Hit Man* by John Perkins — Preface, Parts I-V.

## The Three Frameworks

### Framework A: The EHM System (Preface, Parts I-III)

EHMs use fraudulent economic forecasts to justify massive World Bank loans. The loans require that US corporations build the infrastructure. The money returns to the US. The debtor country is left with debt it can never repay. Then the IMF arrives and demands privatization, resource access, and political loyalty.

> **Case: Claudine's Training** (Chapter 3): "Your job is to cheat countries around the globe out of billions of dollars. You encourage world leaders to become part of a vast network that promotes US commercial interests. In the end, they become ensnared in a web of debt that ensures their loyalty."

### Framework B: The Corporatocracy (Chapters 3, 14, 46)

Not a single conspiracy but a network: corporations, banks, governments, and wealthy families with aligned interests. Example: Robert McNamara moved from Ford CEO → Secretary of Defense → World Bank President.

### Framework C: Death Economy vs. Life Economy (Chapters 46-47)

Death economy: fear, debt, extraction, consumption, war. Life economy: trust, sustainability, regeneration, community, peace. The transition requires individual and collective action.
