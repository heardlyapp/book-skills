# Anti-Patterns · What Blocks Happiness

> Source: *The Happiness Advantage*

---

## The Success → Happiness Fallacy

"I'll be happy when I get the promotion / buy the house / find the partner." Research shows every success raises happiness temporarily, then returns to baseline.

**Fix:** Reverse the formula. Raise your baseline happiness first through gratitude and positivity practices.

## The Negativity Bias

Your brain evolved to scan for threats. In modern life, this means you naturally notice what's wrong before what's right.

**Fix:** The Tetris Effect — train your brain to scan for positive patterns through daily gratitude practice.

## The Overwhelm Trap

Trying to change everything at once. Diet, exercise, work habits, relationships — all at the same time.

**Fix:** The Zorro Circle. Pick one small area. Master it. Expand.

## Social Isolation

When stressed, people withdraw. But social connection is the #1 predictor of resilience.

**Fix:** Treat social investment like any other priority. Schedule it. Protect it.
