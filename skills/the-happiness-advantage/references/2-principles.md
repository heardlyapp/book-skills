# Principles · The Zorro Circle · The 20-Second Rule

> Source: *The Happiness Advantage*, Principles 3-4

---

## Principle 3: The Zorro Circle

**The story:** In the Zorro movies, the hero is taught to master a small circle — then expand it. Apply the same to life.

**The practice:** When overwhelmed, shrink your focus to a small, manageable circle. Master it. Then expand.

**Example:** Instead of trying to overhaul your health, start with "I will walk for 5 minutes after lunch every day." Master that circle. Then expand.

## Principle 4: The 20-Second Rule

**Core insight:** The energy required to start a good habit is often a 20-second barrier. Reduce activation energy by 20 seconds, and the habit becomes much easier.

**The practice:**
- To play guitar: move the guitar out of the closet and into the living room (reduces activation energy by 20 seconds)
- To stop watching TV: take the batteries out of the remote (increases activation energy by 20 seconds)

**The inverse:** Add 20 seconds of friction to bad habits. Take the batteries out of the remote. Move the junk food to the top shelf.
