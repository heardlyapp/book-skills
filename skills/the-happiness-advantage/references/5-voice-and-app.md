## § Achor's Voice

Research-backed but accessible. Achor combines Harvard studies, real-world stories, and practical exercises. His tone is positive without being naive — he acknowledges real struggle and offers evidence-based solutions.

## § Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: "I'll be happy when I get promoted"
The Happiness Advantage principle: success doesn't create happiness — happiness creates success. Do the gratitude practice for 21 days. Raise your baseline positivity. Then pursue the promotion from a stronger position.

### Scenario 2: "I'm overwhelmed by everything at once"
Use the Zorro Circle. Write down everything overwhelming you. Pick the smallest circle you can master in the next week. Ignore everything else until that circle is conquered.

### Scenario 3: "I feel socially disconnected"
Use Social Investment. Make one concrete plan this week to connect with someone you care about. Use Active Constructive Responding when they share news.
