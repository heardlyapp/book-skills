# Core Framework · The Happiness Advantage · The Tetris Effect

> Source: *The Happiness Advantage*, Principles 1-2

---

## Principle 1: The Happiness Advantage

**Core research:** When the brain is positive, intelligence rises, creativity rises, energy rises. A positive brain is 31% more productive than a negative, neutral, or stressed brain.

**The wrong formula:** "If I work hard, I'll succeed; if I succeed, I'll be happy." This formula means happiness is always in the future. The research shows the opposite: happiness fuels success.

**The Fulcrum and the Lever:** Your mindset (the fulcrum) determines the power of your effort (the lever). Move the fulcrum toward positivity, and your effort generates exponentially more results.

## Principle 2: The Tetris Effect

**The research:** After playing Tetris for days, people start seeing Tetris patterns everywhere — in sleep, in their environment. The brain trains itself to scan for patterns.

**The application:** Train your brain to scan for patterns of opportunity and positivity instead of threats and negativity. If you look for the negative, you'll find it. If you look for the positive, you'll find that too.

**The 21-Day Gratitude Practice:** Write down 3 new things you're grateful for each day for 21 days. This retrains the brain to scan for positive patterns first. After 21 days, the effect persists even after you stop.
