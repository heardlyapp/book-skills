## The Law of Attraction — Foundational Principle

The Law of Attraction is described as the most powerful law in the universe. It governs everything from the formation of galaxies to the circumstances of your daily life. Just as the law of gravity is always operating whether you believe in it or not, the Law of Attraction is perpetually active. Every thought you think has a specific frequency. When you think a thought, that thought goes out into the universe and magnetically attracts all things on the same frequency. Those matching-frequency things then materialize in your life.

**Key teaching:** Your current life is an exact reflection of your dominant thoughts. If you want to change your life, you must change your thoughts. This is not positive thinking as a surface-level exercise — it is about fundamentally shifting the frequency you broadcast to the universe.

Rhonda Byrne uses the metaphor of a broadcast tower: your thoughts and feelings are the signal you transmit. Every person, circumstance, and event you experience is a "program" that has arrived on that frequency. If you don't like the program you're receiving, you can't change it by fighting the TV. You must change the channel — change your frequency.

## The Creative Process — 3 Steps

### Step 1: Ask (Get Clear)

"You get clear about what you want."

The universe responds to your command, but you must first know what you want. Most people don't get what they want because they don't know what they want — or worse, they know what they don't want and broadcast that instead.

**How to Ask properly:**
- Be specific and clear. Not "I want more money" but "I receive $50,000 by December 31st"
- Ask in the present tense as if you already have it
- Write it down. Speak it aloud. Make it real
- Use visual aids — cut out pictures, create a vision board

**Common mistake:** Asking and then immediately doubting. "I ask for $50,000 but how can that possibly happen?" This contradictory broadcast cancels out the request.

### Step 2: Believe (Act As If)

"Your thoughts are the primary cause of your life. Act as if you already have what you want."

This is the step most people fail at. They ask, but their belief system is unchanged. They still feel like someone who doesn't have what they've asked for. The gap between asking and receiving is always filled by belief — or by doubt.

**How to Believe properly:**
- Act, speak, and think as if you are already receiving what you've asked for
- If you want wealth, feel the joy of abundance now. Buy a wallet that looks full. Write checks to yourself
- If you want a relationship, prepare for it. Clear space in your closet. Sleep in the middle of the bed to leave room
- Eliminate contradictory thoughts immediately. When doubt arises, choose faith
- The universe cannot send you what you haven't believed you can receive

**The paradox:** If you believe you have it, you don't need the proof. If you need proof, you don't believe.

### Step 3: Receive (Feel Good Now)

"Feel the way you will feel once your desire has manifested."

Receiving is about alignment. You must tune yourself to the frequency of already having what you want. How do you know you're in the receiving state? You feel good. Joy, love, gratitude, anticipation, excitement — these are receiving emotions.

**How to Receive properly:**
- Feel the feeling of having your desire right now
- Do things that make you feel good. Put yourself in a state of joy and appreciation
- The universe responds to how you feel, not just what you think
- Be receptive. Be open. The "how" is not your job — it's the universe's job
- Expect good things. Expect that your desire is on its way

## Like Attracts Like — The Mechanism

The universe operates on the principle of attraction, not separation. Every proton and electron in every atom participates in this dance of attraction. You are a collection of atoms — you are a magnet.

**The three forces of the universe:** Creation, Preservation, Destruction — or in Hinduism: Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva. Everything cycles through creation, preservation, and destruction. Your thoughts create, your consistent attention preserves, and your letting go allows destruction of old patterns.

**The key insight:** You attract what you think about most, not what you want most. There is a profound difference. A person can desperately want wealth but think more about their poverty. What dominates your consciousness is what you attract.

### Case Study 1: Colin at Disney World

Colin, a 10-year-old boy, went to Disney World with his family. Before going, Colin deliberately applied The Secret. He imagined himself and his family being chosen as "First Family" — the family selected for special VIP treatment, getting walk-on passes to every ride without waiting in line. Colin visualized this experience in detail. He talked about it with absolute certainty. When his family arrived at Disney World, a park employee approached them and announced that they had been randomly chosen as that day's "First Family." Colin's family received VIP walk-on passes to every ride. Colin told his mother later that he knew they would be chosen — he had already seen it in his mind.

**Lesson:** Colin had no doubt. He wasn't hoping or wishing. He knew. The clear knowing of a child — untainted by adult skepticism — is a powerful demonstration of belief in action.

### Case Study 2: Jeannie's Mail Story

Jeannie, a woman who was studying the Law of Attraction, found herself accidentally receiving mail addressed to Bob Proctor — one of the most famous teachers of The Secret. She had never met Bob Proctor. She didn't know where he lived. Yet somehow, through the operation of the Law of Attraction, his mail ended up in her hands. This synchronicity led to a chain of events that eventually resulted in her meeting Bob Proctor in person — a meeting that significantly advanced her understanding and application of The Secret.

**Lesson:** This illustrates how the Law of Attraction orchestrates "coincidences" that seem improbable from a logical standpoint. When you are aligned with a frequency, the universe moves circumstances, people, and events to bring you into contact with what you need. There are no coincidences — only attraction.

### Case Study 3: John Assaraf's Vision Board

John Assaraf, one of the teachers featured in both the book and the film, created a vision board featuring a picture of a specific house he wanted to own. He visualized himself living in that house, feeling the feeling of ownership. He then forgot about the board entirely. Five years later, after a series of moves and life changes, John found himself living in a house. One day, he found the old vision board and realized — that exact house was the one he was now living in. He had not consciously tried to find that house. He had not remembered the picture on his vision board. Yet the Law of Attraction had guided him, step by step, to that specific house with those exact features.

**Lesson:** This case demonstrates that you don't need to constantly revisit your visualization. Setting a clear intention in your subconscious and in the universe, then letting go of the "how," allows the universe to arrange the details. The vision board served as a clear command; forgetting about it allowed it to manifest without resistance.

## Common Pitfalls with the Creative Process

1. **Asking from a place of lack.** When you ask from desperation, you broadcast the frequency of not having. The universe responds with more not-having.

2. **Doubting after asking.** Asking once and then spending the rest of your day thinking about why it won't happen is like planting a seed and then digging it up every hour to check on it.

3. **Trying to control the "how."** The most frequent cause of failure. You ask, then you try to figure out how it will happen. Your job is the "what." The universe handles the "how."

4. **Forcing without feeling.** Going through the motions of the creative process without actually feeling the reality of having your desire. This is mechanical and produces no attraction.

5. **Giving up too soon.** The universe doesn't operate on your timeline. Time is an illusion. Your desire already exists in its completed form — you need to hold your frequency long enough for it to materialize in your physical reality.

## Quick Application Steps

1. Identify one specific thing you want to attract
2. Write it down in present tense: "I now have/am/experience [X]"
3. Close your eyes and feel the feeling of already having it for 3-5 minutes
4. Throughout the day, whenever you think about it, shift to feeling the joy of possession
5. Do not think about how it will come. Release the "how"
6. Take inspired action when it arises — but only inspired action, not forced action from worry
7. Practice gratitude for everything it in your life as if you already have it
8. Expect it to arrive. Go to sleep knowing it's on its way
