## Philosophy (7 Rules to Remember)

1. **Your thoughts create your reality.** Every thought has a frequency. Whatever you think about most, you attract. The Law of Attraction is always operating — whether you're aware of it or not.
2. **Like attracts like.** You are a living magnet. Your dominant thoughts and feelings broadcast a frequency that attracts matching circumstances, people, and events into your life.
3. **The feeling is what matters, not just the thought.** Pure thought has no power to attract. The feeling behind the thought — the emotion you experience when you imagine having what you want — is what creates the attraction.
4. **The universe responds to your command.** You are the creator of your own universe. Ask clearly, believe you've received, and receive by feeling good now. The universe handles the "how."
5. **Gratitude is the most powerful process.** Being grateful for what you have now attracts more things to be grateful for. Gratitude shifts your frequency to one of abundance and opens the channel to receive.
6. **Don't focus on what you don't want.** The Law of Attraction doesn't process negatives. If you think "I don't want debt," the universe only hears "debt." You attract what you think about most.
7. **Your life is in your hands.** No one and nothing else is responsible for your circumstances. This is not about blame — it's about power. The same power that created your current situation can transform it.

## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **The Law of Attraction**: Like attracts like. Your dominant thoughts become your life. It is always working, whether you know it or not.
- **The Creative Process (3 Steps)**: Ask (get clear on what you want), Believe (act as if you've already received it), Receive (feel the feeling of having it now).
- **Like Attracts Like**: Everything in the universe has a frequency. Your thoughts and feelings broadcast a frequency that attracts matching conditions.
- **The Secret**: Hidden wisdom that has appeared in fragments throughout human history — in oral tradition, in literature, in religions and philosophies. Now revealed openly.
- **Gratitude**: The most powerful practice. Being grateful for what you have now opens the channel for more to come. The Gratitude Rock method.
- **Visualization**: Create mental pictures with as much detail and movement as possible. The mind cannot distinguish between what you vividly imagine and what is real.
- **Power of Feeling**: Feeling good is the highest-order attractor. Joy, love, gratitude, appreciation, passion — these frequencies attract positive conditions.
- **The "Don't Want" Epidemic**: Most people focus on what they don't want and wonder why they keep getting it. The universe doesn't process negatives.
- **Expectation**: Desire connects you to what you want. Expectation draws it into your life. Expect good things to come.
- **Action**: Take action when inspired. Not from worry, fear, or force — but from inspired knowing. The universe guides you to the right actions.

## 7 Key Principles

1. **You Create Your Own Universe.** Every aspect of your life is drawn to you by your dominant thoughts. This is not something you "believe in" — it is a law of nature, like gravity. Change your thoughts, change your life.

2. **Ask, Believe, Receive.** The three-step creative process is the precise mechanism of manifestation. First, get clear on what you want (Ask). Second, act as if it's already yours (Believe). Third, feel the joy of having it now (Receive). The "how" is not your job.

3. **Gratitude Is the Master Key.** Gratitude is not merely nice — it is the most powerful action you can take. When you are grateful for what you have, the universe delivers more. Use a gratitude rock, keep a daily gratitude list, and say "thank you" hundreds of times a day as Einstein did.

4. **Feelings Create Attraction.** It is not enough to think about what you want. You must feel the feeling of having it. The feeling is the secret within The Secret. Your feelings are your guidance system — when you feel good, you are on the right frequency.

5. **Do Not Give Attention to What You Don't Want.** The "Don't Want" epidemic is the single biggest mistake people make. They talk about their debts, think about their illnesses, worry about their problems — and attract more of them. Focus only on what you do want.

6. **Visualize with Feeling and Movement.** Create a vivid scene in your mind that implies you already have your desire. Add movement and detail. The mind cannot distinguish imagination from reality. For best results, do this just before sleep as Neville Goddard taught.

7. **Your Life Is Your Responsibility.** Neither blame nor credit goes to anyone else. You attract everything that comes into your life — the good and the seemingly bad. This gives you total power to change any situation by changing your thoughts about it.

## Historical Roots & Philosophical Context

The Secret draws on a lineage of New Thought philosophy, Hermetic principles, and esoteric wisdom dating back thousands of years. The book traces fragments of the Law of Attraction through:

- **Ancient Traditions**: Hinduism (the universe as illusion, consciousness creates reality), Buddhism (mind precedes all things), Hermeticism (the Principle of Correspondence — as above, so below)
- **Religious Texts**: The Bible ("Ask and it shall be given you," "According to your faith be it unto you"), the Kabbalah, Sufi poetry of Rumi
- **Philosophy**: Plato (the Forms as ultimate reality), Emerson (the Oversoul, the law of compensation), William James (the power of belief)
- **The New Thought Movement**: Prentice Mulford (1870s), Charles Haanel's *The Master Key System* (1912), Wallace Wattles' *The Science of Getting Rich* (1910), Neville Goddard, Christian D. Larson, Genevieve Behrend
- **Modern Teachers Featured in the Book**: Bob Proctor, Dr. Joe Vitale, Jack Canfield, John Assaraf, Michael Bernard Beckwith, Lee Brower, Dr. John Demartini, Marie Diamond, Mike Dooley, Bob Doyle, Hale Dwoskin, Morris Goodman, Dr. John Gray, Dr. John Hagelin, Bill Harris, Dr. Ben Johnson, Loral Langemeier, Lisa Nichols, James Ray, David Schirmer, Marci Shimoff, Dr. Denis Waitley, Neale Donald Walsch, Dr. Fred Alan Wolf

## Key Stories from the Book

- **Jeannie's Mail Story**: Jeannie accidentally received Bob Proctor's mail, leading to an in-person meeting — illustrating how the Law of Attraction arranges coincidences
- **Colin at Disney World**: A 10-year-old boy used The Secret at Disney World and his family was chosen as First Family with VIP walk-on passes to every ride
- **John Assaraf's Vision Board**: Created a vision board with a picture of a dream home, forgot about it, and 5 years later found himself living in that exact house
- **Joe Sugarman's Parking Spaces**: Uses gratitude for parking spaces and credits gratitude for all his success
- **Gratitude Rocks Story**: A South African man gave his son a gratitude rock that coincidentally healed the son, then the man started selling them for charity
- **Olympic Visual Motor Rehearsal**: Study showed the same muscles fire during mental rehearsal of Olympic events as during actual physical performance
- **Einstein's Gratitude Practice**: Einstein said "thank you" hundreds of times daily — he knew The Secret
- **Neville Goddard's Revision**: The Pruning Shears of Revision — replay your day in its ideal form before sleep
- **The Woman Who Ran from Cancer**: A woman visualized herself with perfect health and stopped focusing on the cancer, which then disappeared
