## The 5-Step Eat Dirt Program

This is the core actionable protocol of the book. Each step builds on the previous one. Dr. Axe recommends keeping a food journal to track emotions, physical symptoms, and bowel movements throughout the process.

### Step 1: Remove — Clear the Kitchen, Clear the Gut

The first and most critical step. Get a garbage bag and throw away everything on the gut grenades list:

| Remove This | And Replace With |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Wheat and all gluten-containing grains | Coconut flour, almond flour, sprouted buckwheat/quinoa |
| Commercial cow's milk (pasteurized, homogenized) | Raw goat's milk kefir, coconut milk, almond milk |
| Refined sugar and high-fructose corn syrup | Raw local honey, manuka honey, stevia, dates |
| Hydrogenated oils (canola, soybean, corn, vegetable) | Coconut oil, ghee, olive oil (cold), flaxseed oil |
| Artificial sweeteners (Equal, Sweet'N Low, Splenda) | Green leaf stevia, raw honey |
| GMO foods (conventional corn, soy) | All organic produce |
| Chlorinated/fluoridated tap water | Filtered or spring water |
| Processed meats with nitrites, MSG, preservatives | Grass-fed beef, wild-caught fish, pastured chicken |

**Important distinction:** Sprouted grains are acceptable for mild to moderate leaky gut. For severe cases, eliminate all grains until the gut heals.

### Step 2: Reseed — Introduce Beneficial Microbes

Start with a **soil-based organism (SBO) probiotic** containing:
- **Bacillus subtilis** — heat-resistant endospore that kills salmonella and other pathogens, supports gut lining healing
- **Bacillus coagulans** — improves nutrient absorption, reduces inflammation, shown to relieve arthritis symptoms (BMC Complementary and Alternative Medicine)

**Beyond supplements, reseed through microexposures:**
- Walk barefoot outside for 20 minutes daily
- Shop at farmer's market for unwashed organic produce
- Play with your dog (owning a dog cuts allergy risk by 50%)
- Consume 1 tbsp raw local honey daily
- Dig in garden soil with bare hands
- Eat 2 servings of fermented foods daily (goat's milk kefir, sauerkraut, kimchi)
- Add medicinal mushrooms (shiitake, reishi, lion's mane, cordyceps, turkey tail)
- Swim in the ocean or freshwater lakes

**The Budwig Diet for constipation** (Dr. Axe's mother's protocol):
- 6 oz raw goat's milk kefir
- 3 tbsp sprouted flax meal
- 2 tbsp flaxseed oil
- Stevia to taste
- Blend and drink daily

### Step 3: Restore — Rebuild the Gut Lining

**Start with a 3-day bone broth fast** to reboot the digestive system and give the gut time to repair. Then add:

| Food | Gut-Healing Property |
|------|----------------------|
| Bone broth (chicken or beef) | Collagen, proline, glycine, glutamine — direct fuel for intestinal cells |
| Organic vegetables (steamed if severe) | Prebiotic fiber, vitamins, minerals, antioxidants |
| Wild-caught salmon | Omega-3s (reduce inflammation), vitamin D, vitamin B12 |
| Grass-fed beef and lamb | Conjugated linolenic acid (CLA), zinc, iron |
| Fermented dairy (goat's milk kefir, yogurt) | Lactobacillus acidophilus, bifidobacteria, vitamin B12, calcium |
| Fermented vegetables (sauerkraut, kimchi) | Lactic acid bacteria, enzymes, digestive aids |
| Coconut products (oil, flour, milk, kefir) | Lauric acid (antiviral/antifungal), medium-chain fatty acids |
| Sprouted seeds (chia, flax, hemp) | Omega-3s, prebiotic fiber for beneficial bacteria |

**Prebiotic fiber foods** (feed the good bacteria): artichokes, asparagus, avocados, beans, blueberries, broccoli, cabbage, carrots, chia seeds, flaxseeds, garlic, kale, lentils, oatmeal, onions, pears, pumpkin, spinach.

### Step 4: Release — Reduce Stress

Techniques ordered by effectiveness for gut healing:
1. **Massage or reflexology** — Reduces cortisol, relieves pain, improves parasympathetic nervous system function. MRI studies show lasting brain changes related to stress response.
2. **Exercise** — Any movement (hiking, biking, swimming, rock climbing) releases endorphins and improves circulation.
3. **Chamomile tea** — Natural antispasmodic, relaxes the entire body including the gut, reduces IBS symptoms.
4. **Essential oil aromatherapy** — Lavender, vetiver, roman chamomile, vanilla, orange, ylang-ylang. A controlled study found aromatherapy increased sleep quality and reduced anxiety in ICU patients.
5. **Forest bathing (shinrin-yoku)** — Japanese practice of walking in the woods and breathing deeply. Phytoncides (trees' essential oils) decrease cortisol, lower blood pressure, enhance immune function, stabilize nervous system.
6. **Magnesium supplement** (~500mg daily) — Relieves tense muscles and headaches. Eat magnesium-rich foods: avocado, pumpkin seeds, spinach, figs, yogurt.
7. **Singing for 10 minutes daily** — Releases deep-rooted stress, improves memory, focus, and mood (The Gerontologist).

### Step 5: Reseal — Close the Gut Tight Junctions for Good

**Key supplements:**
- **L-glutamine** (5g twice daily with meals) — Fuel for small intestine cells, helps thicken cell walls, resist Staphylococcus infections (Antimicrobial Agents and Chemotherapy). Best form: l-alanyl-glutamine.
- **Digestive enzymes** (1-2 capsules per meal) — Full-spectrum with protease (breaks down gluten), amylase (starches), lipase (fats), lactase (dairy). University of Salerno study: significantly improved bloating, flatulence, and abdominal pain in IBS patients.
- **Licorice root** — Adaptogenic herb that supports adrenal glands and protects the gut lining.
- **Probiotics** (high-quality live strains) — Continue throughout reseal phase.
- **Bentonite clay** (1 tsp in water twice daily) — Binds to toxins and pulls them out of the body. Dr. Axe gargles with it daily.

## Gut Type-Specific Protocols (Part 3)

### Candida Gut Protocol
- **Avoid:** Sugar, fruit (except berries), vinegar, fermented foods, mushrooms, yeast, alcohol, peanuts
- **Eat:** Coconut oil (3 tbsp daily — lauric acid kills candida), garlic, ginger, oregano oil, caprylic acid, pau d'arco
- **Supplements:** Oregano oil, caprylic acid, berberine, grapefruit seed extract, spirulina (anticandida)
- **Key principle:** Starve the yeast by eliminating all sugar, then kill remaining candida with antifungal foods and supplements

### Stressed Gut Protocol
- **Avoid:** Caffeine, alcohol, sugar, processed foods
- **Eat:** Adaptogenic herbs (ashwagandha, rhodiola, maca), warm cooked foods, bone broth, healthy fats
- **Supplements:** Magnesium, ashwagandha, rhodiola, holy basil, phosphatidylserine, vitamin B complex
- **Key principle:** The gut-brain axis means healing the gut requires healing the stress response. Rest and adrenal support are non-negotiable.

### Immune Gut Protocol
- **Avoid:** All common allergens (gluten, dairy, eggs, soy, corn, nightshades), environmental chemicals
- **Eat:** Bone broth (daily), fermented vegetables, wild-caught salmon, coconut kefir, cruciferous vegetables
- **Supplements:** Medicinal mushrooms (reishi, turkey tail — NK cells increased tremendously in 6 weeks), L-glutamine, quercetin, frankincense oil
- **Key principle:** The immune system has been trained to overreact. Calm it down by removing triggers and supporting regulatory T cells with mushrooms

### Gastric Gut Protocol
- **Avoid:** Raw vegetables, high-fiber foods initially, spicy foods, alcohol, carbonated beverages
- **Eat:** Bone broth (frequent small amounts), cooked vegetables, lean protein, coconut oil, aloe vera juice
- **Supplements:** Betaine HCl (to increase stomach acid — SIBO is caused by low acid, not high acid), digestive enzymes, L-glutamine, slippery elm, marshmallow root
- **Key principle:** Most acid reflux is caused by too little stomach acid, not too much. Restoring proper stomach acid levels eliminates SIBO

### Toxic Gut Protocol
- **Avoid:** Processed foods, alcohol, fried foods, conventional meats, synthetic fragrances
- **Eat:** Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cabbage, kale), beets, artichokes, dandelion greens, milk thistle tea, lemon water
- **Supplements:** Milk thistle (silymarin), NAC, chlorella/spirulina (bind heavy metals), activated charcoal, bentonite clay, liposomal glutathione, vitamin C
- **Key principle:** The liver is overwhelmed by toxins leaking through the gut. Support the liver's detox pathways first, then heal the gut
