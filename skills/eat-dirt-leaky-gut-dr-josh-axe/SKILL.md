## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **Leaky Gut (Increased Intestinal Permeability):** The gut lining's tight junctions loosen, allowing toxins, undigested food particles, and microbes to leak into the bloodstream, triggering systemic inflammation.
- **Zonulin:** The only known protein that controls tight junction opening/closing. Triggered by gluten exposure and gut bacteria imbalance. Discovered by Dr. Alessio Fasano (2000).
- **The 85/15 Rule:** A healthy gut maintains roughly 85% beneficial/neutral bacteria and 15% pathogenic bacteria. Modern lifestyle has flipped this ratio in most people.
- **The Old Friends Theory:** Our bodies evolved alongside billions of microbes. When we sanitize them away, our immune system loses its teachers and goes haywire — attacking itself.
- **Five Gut Types:** Candida Gut (yeast overgrowth, sugar cravings), Stressed Gut (adrenal fatigue, hormone imbalance), Immune Gut (allergies, autoimmune), Gastric Gut (SIBO, acid reflux), Toxic Gut (gallbladder issues, skin problems).
- **The 5 Rs: Remove → Reseed → Restore → Release → Reseal.** The core healing sequence — remove toxins and trigger foods, reseed with probiotics, restore with bone broth and nutrient-dense foods, release stress, and reseal the gut lining with targeted supplements.

## Key Principles (7 Principles)

1. **Remove the gut grenades first.** Gluten, pasteurized dairy, refined sugar, hydrogenated oils, and GMO foods directly damage the gut lining. Clear your kitchen before starting any protocol.
2. **Reseed with soil-based organisms daily.** The best probiotics come from dirt — look for Bacillus subtilis and Bacillus coagulans strains in shelf-stable SBO supplements. These are not the same as refrigerated probiotic pills.
3. **Restore the gut lining with bone broth.** Collagen, proline, glycine, and glutamine from slow-simmered bones heal and seal the intestinal wall. A 3-day bone broth fast at the start of the program can reboot your gut.
4. **Release stress or nothing else works.** Emotional stress directly triggers zonulin release and decimates probiotic diversity. Massage, forest bathing, chamomile tea, and essential oils are non-negotiable parts of the protocol.
5. **Microexposures beat megadoses.** Small, daily exposures to beneficial microbes (from organic carrots rinsed not scrubbed, raw local honey, paw-dirt from your dog, barefoot walks) are more effective than occasional large doses.
6. **Heal by gut type, not by generic protocol.** The five gut types (Candida, Stressed, Immune, Gastric, Toxic) have different root causes and require different foods and supplements. Take the quiz and customize.
7. **Reseal the gut before you need medication.** L-glutamine (5g twice daily), digestive enzymes (with every meal), and licorice root (adaptogenic adrenal support) are the key supplements for closing the tight junctions for good.
