# 2 — Principles

> Source: *Love Yourself First* by Marc Reklau.

## 7 Principles

### 1. Take 100% Responsibility

"Blaming others for your problems is a waste of time." You can't change others. You can change how you respond.

### 2. You Are Not Your Actions

"You can fail at something without being a failure." Separating what you do from who you are is essential for healthy self-esteem.

> **Example: The Misstep at Work** (Part II): You made a mistake at work. That doesn't make you a bad employee or a bad person. It means you made a mistake. Period.

### 3. The Inner Critic Can Be Changed

The inner voice is not objective truth. It's a habit. You can develop a new habit of self-compassion.

### 4. You Are Enough — Right Now

Not when you reach X goal. Not when you lose the weight. Right now. "Self-concept is destiny."

### 5. Stop Comparing

"There will always be someone smarter, richer, and more successful." Comparison is a game you can never win.

### 6. Happiness is a Choice

> **Example: The Two Salesmen** (Part IV): Two salesmen lose their jobs. One sees it as a disaster. The other sees it as an opportunity to start his own business. Same external event, different internal choice.

### 7. Self-Love Comes First

"Love yourself first." Not selfishly. But if you don't love yourself, you have nothing to give others.
