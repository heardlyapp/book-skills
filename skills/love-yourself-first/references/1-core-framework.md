# 1 — Core Framework

> Source: *Love Yourself First* by Marc Reklau — Parts I-IV.

## The Three Frameworks

### Framework A: The 100% Responsibility Principle (Part I)

The foundation of everything: "You are the only one responsible for your life." Stop complaining, stop blaming, stop waiting for others to change. Take full ownership.

### Framework B: True vs. False Self-Esteem (Parts II-III)

False self-esteem: "I'm worthy because I achieved X." True self-esteem: "I'm worthy — period." The key distinction: "You are not your actions." You can fail at something without being a failure.

> **Example: The Inner Dialogue Shift** (Part I, Day 13): "Keep your word. If you say you're going to do something, do it. If you make a promise, keep it." Each completed commitment builds self-trust. Self-esteem comes from keeping promises to yourself first.

### Framework C: Happiness as a Choice (Part IV)

"Happiness is a choice." Not a result of external circumstances. "When you make the decision to be happy, your brain starts to look for reasons to be happy." Gratitude rewires the brain.

> **Example: The Gratitude Practice** (Part IV, Day x): Write 3 things you're grateful for every day. This simple practice trains the brain to scan for positivity. Studies show it measurably increases well-being.
