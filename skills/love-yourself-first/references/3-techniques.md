# 3 — Techniques

> Source: *Love Yourself First* by Marc Reklau.

## 5 Tools

### 1. The Mirror Practice

Look yourself in the eyes for 30 seconds. Say: "I love you. I accept you exactly as you are." Do this daily.

### 2. The Inner Critic Name Game

Give your inner critic a name (like "The Judge" or "Gremlin"). When you hear the critical voice, say: "That's just The Judge talking."

> **Example: The Judge in Action** (Part I, Day 11): The inner critic says: "You'll never be good enough." Name it: "That's The Judge." Then respond: "Actually, I am growing every day, and that is enough."

### 3. The Gratitude Journal

Write 3 things you're grateful for every day. This trains your brain to look for positivity.

> **Example: The Brain Rewiring** (Part IV): "When I started writing 3 gratitudes daily, within 2 weeks I noticed my brain automatically scanning for things to be grateful for throughout the day."

### 4. The Self-Acceptance Stance

When you notice harsh self-judgment, place your hand on your heart and say: "I am enough. I don't have to be perfect."

### 5. The 30-Day Commitment

Pick one practice. Commit for 30 days. The habit will form.
