# 5 — Voice & App

> Source: *Love Yourself First* by Marc Reklau.

## Voice

Reklau writes like a supportive coach — direct, encouraging, and relentlessly practical. Each chapter is short and ends with an action step. The 30-day format makes it digestible.

> **Example: The 30-Day Format** (Book Structure): 30 short chapters, each with one concrete practice. Day 1: Stop complaining. Day 2: Take responsibility. Builds momentum.

> **Example: The "Give Up Guilt" Chapter** (Part I, Day 10): Guilt and shame are not productive. Acknowledge the mistake, learn, and move on.

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Low self-esteem** — Mirror practice, gratitude journal, inner critic name game
2. **Perfectionism** — The "I am enough" stance, accept imperfection
3. **People-pleasing** — Learn to say no, fulfill your own needs first
4. **Inner critic** — Name it, challenge it, replace it
5. **Happiness deferred** — Choose happiness now, gratitude practice
