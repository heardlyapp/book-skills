# Techniques: Pride and Connection

> Source: The Power of Moments, Chapters 7-10

## Pride — Milestones and Courage

Moments of pride come from recognition of achievement and from having the courage to take a difficult step.

> **Case: The First Step** (Chapter 7): A man who'd been afraid of heights for decades finally climbs a small rock wall. The moment of courage changes his self-perception. The pride came from the response-ability — the choice to act despite fear.

> **Key takeaway**: Pride comes from responding to a challenge. Give people the opportunity to choose courage.

## Connection — Shared Moments

Shared experiences create bonds, especially when they involve vulnerability, purpose, or struggle.

> **Case: The Shared Struggle** (Chapter 9): Groups that face a difficult challenge together — like Outward Bound or team boot camps — bond more deeply than groups that only succeed together. Shared struggle creates powerful connection.

> **Key takeaway**: Connection is created through shared vulnerability and purpose. Don't smooth away difficulty — share it.

## Creating Defining Moments

Putting it all together: designing moments that combine multiple EPIC elements.

> **Case: The Transformational Trip** (Chapter 10): A family creates a "trip of a lifetime" that combines new experiences (Elevation), self-discoveries (Insight), achievements (Pride), and shared time (Connection).

> **Key takeaway**: Shared struggle and vulnerability create deeper bonds than shared success.
