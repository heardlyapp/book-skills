# The Vatican Connection

> Source: Operation Gladio, Chapters 3, 5, 6, 11, 14-17

## The Vatican as Financial Hub

Williams alleges that the Vatican Bank (IOR) was the financial clearinghouse for Gladio operations.

### Key Players

| Person | Role |
|--------|------|
| Pope Pius XII | Allegedly formed alliance with CIA |
| Cardinal Spellman | CIA operative, Knights of Malta |
| Archbishop Marcinkus | Head of IOR, from Cicero, Illinois |
| Michele Sindona | "The pope's banker," Mafia-CIA-Vatican nexus |
| Roberto Calvi | Head of Banco Ambrosiano, died under Blackfriars Bridge |

> **Case: Sindona — The Pope's Banker** (Chapters 6, 9, 14)
> Michele Sindona was a Sicilian financier who became the nexus between the Vatican, the CIA, and the Mafia. He was introduced to the Vatican by the Gambino family and quickly became the financial advisor to the IOR. He acquired banks in Italy, the US, and Switzerland, laundering money from heroin sales and covert operations. When his empire collapsed in 1974, he staged a fake kidnapping in 1979 to avoid prosecution. His threat to reveal Vatican secrets forced the IOR to pay 300 billion lire in hush money. He was poisoned in prison in 1986.
> **Key takeaway:** Sindona's story illustrates the intertwining of religious, criminal, and intelligence networks — and what happens when someone who knows too much becomes a liability.

> **Case: Calvi — The Man Who Knew Too Much** (Chapters 14, 16, 18)
> Roberto Calvi, head of Banco Ambrosiano, was the Vatican's primary banker. He set up shell companies in Panama for the IOR and financed covert operations in Latin America. When Ambrosiano collapsed in 1982 with a $1.3 billion hole, Calvi fled to London. He was found hanging under Blackfriars Bridge on June 18, 1982. The initial verdict was suicide, but evidence suggested murder. Calvi's briefcase contained documents threatening to expose Vatican involvement in money laundering and arms trafficking.
> **Key takeaway:** Whether Calvi was murdered or driven to suicide, his death (and the loss of his documents) demonstrates how the Vatican, CIA, and Mafia had become so entangled that the truth was literally killed.
