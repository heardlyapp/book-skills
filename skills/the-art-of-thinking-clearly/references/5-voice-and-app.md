## § Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: "My investment is down 30% — should I hold or sell?"
Beware of two biases: **Sunk Cost** (you've already lost, so you want to wait for it to come back) and **Endowment Effect** (you value what you own more than it's worth). **Ask:** "If I had cash today instead of this stock, would I buy it at the current price?"

### Scenario 2: "I'm about to launch a project — how long will it take?"
You're likely suffering from **Planning Fallacy**. **Fix:** Use reference class forecasting. Find 3 similar projects. How long did they actually take? Use that as your estimate, not your optimistic fantasy.

### Scenario 3: "Everyone is buying this new cryptocurrency"
**Social Proof** and **Herd Mentality** are in full effect. **Fix:** Ask "What is the actual evidence for the value of this asset?" not "Is everyone else doing it?" Also check **Availability** — if it's all over the news, you're overweighing it.
