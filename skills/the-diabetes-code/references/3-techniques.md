# Techniques — The Diabetes Code

## How to Reverse Type 2 Diabetes (Based on Dr. Fung)

### Technique 1: The Sugar Bowl Test
Your body = sugar bowl. When it's full, sugar spills into the blood. Ask: "Am I trying to hide the sugar (with drugs) or get rid of it (with diet)?" This reframes the entire approach to treatment.

### Technique 2: Eliminate Liquid Sugar First
Sugary drinks (soda, juice, sweetened coffee) are the single most damaging source. Fructose in liquid form is metabolized as a liver toxin and bypasses satiety signals. "Stop drinking your sugar." This is the single highest-impact intervention.

### Technique 3: Low-Carb Plate
Fill your plate with: protein (meat, fish, eggs, tofu), natural fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts, butter), and non-starchy vegetables (leafy greens, broccoli, cauliflower). Eliminate: sugar, white flour, white rice, potatoes, sugary drinks. "Eliminate all sugar and refined carbohydrates from your diet."

### Technique 4: The 16-Hour Fast
Eat all your food within an 8-hour window. Skip breakfast (bulletproof coffee optional) and eat lunch at noon, dinner before 8 PM. This gives your body 16 hours to burn stored glucose. "Fasting is the simplest and surest method to force your body to burn sugar."

### Technique 5: The 24-Hour Fast 2-3x/Week
Once adapted, a 24-hour fast (dinner to dinner) burns significantly more stored glucose. Start with 16:8. Progress to 24 hours 2-3 times per week. "If you don't eat, will your blood glucose come down? Of course."

### Technique 6: The "Can't Cure Dietary Disease" Test
When a doctor prescribes a medication that causes weight gain (insulin, sulfonylureas), ask: "Does this drug help eliminate sugar from my body, or does it just hide it?" If the answer is "hide it," find another approach.

### Technique 7: The Seven Trials Test
Seven major trials proved that blood glucose lowering does NOT reduce heart disease in diabetics. When evaluating any diabetes treatment, ask: "Does this treatment reduce my risk of heart disease, blindness, kidney failure, and amputation — or just lower my blood glucose number?"
