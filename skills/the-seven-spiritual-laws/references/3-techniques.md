# 3 — Intention and Desire

> Source: "The Seven Spiritual Laws of Success" by Deepak Chopra

## Law 5: Intention and Desire

Intention is the power behind creation. It works in the field of pure potentiality. Combined with detachment, it's unstoppable.

## The Process

1. Take a silent moment
2. Set a clear intention
3. Release it to the universe
4. Act on opportunities

> **Case: The Garden Metaphor** (Chapter 5): Chopra explains intention like planting a garden. "You plant the seed (intention), you water it (attention), but you don't dig it up every day to check if it's growing (detachment). Your job is to plant and water. The universe does the rest."
> **Key takeaway**: Intention without detachment is grasping. Detachment without intention is passivity. Both are required. The paradox: hold your intention lightly.

> **Case: The Quantum Field** (Chapter on Intention): Chopra describes the universe as a "quantum soup" of all possibilities. "Your intention collapses the wave of possibility into reality. The clearer your intention, the more likely it will manifest."
> **Key takeaway**: Intention is not wishing — it's knowing. The clearer and more focused your intention, the faster it manifests.
