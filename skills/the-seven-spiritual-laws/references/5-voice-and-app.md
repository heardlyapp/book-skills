# 5 — Chopra's Voice + Application

> Source: "The Seven Spiritual Laws of Success" by Deepak Chopra

## Chopra's Voice

Deepak Chopra is a bestselling author and founder of the Chopra Foundation. His voice: eloquent, soothing, intellectually rich. He bridges ancient wisdom with modern science.

> **Case: The Physician Turned Mystic** (Introduction): Chopra was a conventional doctor before he turned to spirituality. "I was trained to see the body as a machine. I came to see it as a field of energy and intelligence." This transition informs his entire approach.
> **Key takeaway**: Chopra's credibility comes from his willingness to challenge his own assumptions. He was a traditional physician before he became a spiritual teacher.

## Key Quote

"Success is not about accumulating more — it's about expressing more of who you truly are."

## 5 Scenarios

1. **Feeling stuck** → Practice silence. The answer is already within you.
2. **Struggling financially** → Practice giving. Give even when you think you can't.
3. **Chasing outcomes** → Practice detachment. Desire strongly, release completely.
4. **Confused about purpose** → Ask: what would I do if I couldn't fail? That's your dharma.
5. **Overwhelmed** → Practice least effort. Accept. Take responsibility. Stop defending.
