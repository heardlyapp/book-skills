# 1 — Pure Potentiality and Dharma

> Source: "The Seven Spiritual Laws of Success" by Deepak Chopra

## Law 1: Pure Potentiality

Your essential nature is pure consciousness — the field of all possibilities. Through silence and meditation, you access this field.

**Practice**: Spend 30 minutes daily in silence. Meditate. Observe without judgment.

## Law 7: Dharma

Everyone has a unique purpose. Life is about expressing your unique talents in service. Purpose + service = fulfillment.

> **Case: The Silence Practice** (Chapter 1): Chopra describes how spending time in silence is not passive — it's the most active thing you can do. "In silence, you access the field of pure potentiality. This is where all creation begins. Without this foundation, the other laws cannot function."
> **Key takeaway**: Silence is not wasted time. It's the foundation of everything else. If you can't be still, you can't access your deepest wisdom.

> **Case: The Purpose Question** (Chapter 7): Chopra asks: "If you had all the money and time in the world, what would you do?" The answer is your dharma. "Your purpose is not something you need to earn or achieve. It's something you need to uncover and express."
> **Key takeaway**: Your purpose is already within you. It's not a question of finding — it's a question of expressing.
