# Key Principles — Future Bright

## I. Intelligence Is Not Fixed — It's Modifiable

"You've been thinking about intelligence in the wrong way." IQ scores fluctuate within a person's lifetime and across generations. The Flynn Effect: IQs rise 3 points per decade. "If genes alone determined IQ, IQs would not rise." Case: Adoption studies show children adopted into higher-SES homes gain 12-16 IQ points.

## II. Intelligence Is the Key Resource of the 21st Century

"Intelligence is now the unrivaled capital resource of the 21st century workforce." The economy demands "symbolic analysts" — workers who gather, interpret, and act on information. Case: The wage premium for college graduates has grown while wages for high school graduates stagnated. "A college degree translates into a 10- to 15-point IQ advantage."

## III. Intelligence Has Three Components

Martinez's model: Fluid Intelligence (Gf — problem-solving), Crystallized Intelligence (Gc — knowledge), and Effective Character (the "smart heart"). "Intelligence is not just about IQ. It includes the dispositions of the heart." Case: London taxi drivers develop enlarged hippocampi through intensive spatial learning — physical brain change from experience.

## IV. Genes Set a Range, but Experience Determines Where You Land

IQ heritability is about 50%. The other 50% is environment — and that includes schooling, home environment, cognitive complexity, nutrition, and stress. Case: The "Brain Boost" project showed that targeted enrichment programs can raise children's IQs.

## V. The Brain Changes With Use (Neuroplasticity)

"The brain is not a static organ. It changes in response to experience." Neurons form new connections, myelin sheaths thicken, brain regions enlarge with use. Case: London taxi drivers' hippocampi grow as they learn "The Knowledge" — the layout of 25,000 city streets.

## VI. Effective Character Makes Intelligence Useful

"Effective character" includes curiosity, openness to experience, persistence (grit), self-regulation, and intellectual humility. Without these, raw intelligence is wasted. "The smart heart" — the motivational and emotional foundations of intelligent action.

## VII. Intelligence Can Be Deliberately Enhanced

Ten specific strategies: broaden experiences, push beyond easy, think critically, master questioning, seek feedback, cultivate effective character, read widely, exercise brain, pursue education, build stimulating relationships. "Intelligence is learnable."
