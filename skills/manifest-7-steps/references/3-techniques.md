# Techniques — Manifest: 7 Steps

## How to Manifest Your Best Life (Based on Roxie Nafousi)

### Technique 1: The Vision Board Creation
Create a physical or digital board with images of what you want to manifest. Put it where you'll see it every day. The key: "When we create an experience in our mind, our brain responds as if it is really happening." Case: Nafousi used a vision board during her manifesting journey — she included images of love, career, and motherhood. All of it came true.

### Technique 2: The Daily Visualization
Spend 5-10 minutes daily visualizing your desired life with all five senses. What does it look like? Feel like? Sound like? Smell like? "If we visualize ourselves in a peaceful setting, our brain triggers the nervous system to calm down and encourages the body to relax." Case: Nafousi visualized her relationship before she met Wade — she pictured the feeling of being loved.

### Technique 3: The Self-Love Mirror Work
Stand in front of a mirror, look yourself in the eyes, and say: "I love you. I believe in you. I am worthy of everything I desire." Daily. "You cannot manifest a life you don't believe you deserve." Case: Nafousi's self-love practice was the foundation of her recovery from addiction and depression.

### Technique 4: The Gratitude Reset
Every morning, write down three specific things you're grateful for. Not generic — specific. "The smell of coffee. The way the light hits my kitchen floor. The text from my friend." The technique: gratitude before desire. Case: Nafousi's consistent gratitude practice raised her baseline vibration and made her receptive to receiving.

### Technique 5: The Envy Investigation
When you feel jealous of someone, don't suppress it. Ask: "What do they have that I want?" That envy is a map to your desires. Use it as inspiration, not comparison. Case: If you're jealous of someone's career, that emotion is telling you what you truly want for yourself.

### Technique 6: The Behaviour Alignment Check
Weekly review: "Is my behavior aligned with what I'm trying to manifest?" If you want a relationship but spend all your time at home alone — misalignment. If you want a promotion but haven't updated your skills — misalignment. Case: Nafousi aligned her behavior by updating her dating profile and going on dates while manifesting love.

### Technique 7: The Surrender Ceremony
After doing all the work, write your intentions on a piece of paper, fold it, and place it somewhere meaningful. Say: "I trust the universe to deliver this in the perfect way, at the perfect time." Then let go. Case: Nafousi's surrender was not giving up — it was releasing the need to control how and when.
