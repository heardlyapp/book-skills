# Key Principles — Manifest: 7 Steps

## I. You Are the Author of Your Own Story

"Manifesting is the ability to create the exact life that you want. It is the ability to draw in anything that you desire and become the author of your own story." Case: Nafousi went from rock bottom — jobless, addicted, depressed — to a successful career, loving relationship, and motherhood. "It looks and feels like magic, and we are all the magicians."

## II. Like Attracts Like (Vibration)

"Everything in the universe is made up of energy. Our thoughts, emotions, and feelings are all made up of energy, too. Different emotions have different frequencies. Like attracts like." Low vibration (fear, doubt, guilt) blocks manifestation. High vibration (love, gratitude, excitement) attracts it.

## III. Self-Love Is the Foundation

"You cannot manifest a life you don't believe you deserve." The most important work of manifestation is internal — cultivating self-worth. Case: Nafousi struggled with addiction and depression because she didn't believe she deserved better. Her transformation required healing that relationship with herself first.

## IV. Clarity Precedes Results

"You can't get to where you want to go if you don't know where it is you are headed." Most people fail at manifestation not because the universe said no — but because they never asked clearly. "Everything is created twice, first in the mind and then in reality" (Robin Sharma).

## V. Behavior Must Align with Intention

"You can't expect different results if you keep doing the same things." Visualization alone is not enough. "Align your behavior" — act as if what you're manifesting is already on its way. Case: Nafousi updated her dating profile, went on dates, made space for love.

## VI. Tests Are Signs of Progress

"When you start manifesting, the universe will test you." Obstacles are not signs that you're wrong — they're signs you're on the right path. "Stay faithful during the storm, and you will be rewarded." Case: Nafousi faced rejections and setbacks before her life transformed.

## VII. Gratitude Opens the Door

"Gratitude raises your vibration immediately." But it's not "I'll be grateful when I get what I want." It's gratitude for what you have right now. "Embrace gratitude without caveats." Case: Nafousi's daily practice of writing three gratitudes every morning changed her vibrational baseline.
