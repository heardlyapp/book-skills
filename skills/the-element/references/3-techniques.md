# Techniques · Luck · Mentors · Practical Steps

> Source: *The Element*, Chapters 7-8

---

## Chapter 7: Do You Feel Lucky?

"Luck is not random. Lucky people create their own luck by being open to opportunity, persistent in the face of rejection, and willing to take risks."

**The 4 luck-increasing practices:**
1. **Be prepared** — Luck favors the prepared. Master your craft.
2. **Be open** — Say yes to things outside your comfort zone
3. **Be persistent** — Most "luck" is persistence meeting opportunity
4. **Be generous** — Help others; luck returns through networks

## Chapter 8: Somebody Help Me

Mentors play a critical role in finding your Element. A mentor:
- Sees potential you don't see in yourself
- Opens doors you couldn't open alone
- Challenges you to be better
- Believes in you before you believe in yourself

**Finding a mentor:** Don't ask "Will you mentor me?" Ask for specific advice on specific challenges. Build the relationship through shared interests.

## Practical Steps to Find Your Element

1. **Explore broadly** — Try things you've never tried. Your Element may be something you haven't encountered yet.
2. **Reflect on childhood** — What did you love doing before you were told it wasn't practical?
3. **Notice when you lose track of time** — That's a signal you're approaching your Element.
4. **Find your people** — Seek communities where your interests are shared and celebrated.
5. **Start small** — You don't need to quit your job tomorrow. Start with evenings and weekends.
